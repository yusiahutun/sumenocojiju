Dolph Lundgren's daughter Ida 'is granted restraining a order against her ex-boyfriend'

Publish date: 2024-03-17

Dolph Lundgren's daughter Ida has reportedly been granted a restraining order against her ex-boyfriend after he threatened her and her family.

A judge has ordered 26-year-old Ida's former partner Jack Eaton-Morris to stay at least 100 yards away from Ida, Dolph, and other family members, reports TMZ. 

According to documents obtained by the publication Jack took her phone and passport off her to stop her leaving a London hotel and grabbed her.

She also claimed that he once threatened to blow up her mother's car and said that he was going to kill himself.
