Hayden Panettiere and baby daughter Kaya star in candid new Instagram photo

Publish date: 2024-08-02

    Kaya Evdokia, the newborn daughter of Hayden Panettiere and Wladimir Klitschko, has starred in her very first Instagram photo. An adorable snapshot shared by proud father Wladimir shows the tiny four-week-old baby sleeping on his lap, while new mum Hayden makes a playful face at the camera. Alongside the photo, the 38-year-old boxer wrote, "#family #klitschko #newyear."

    View post on Instagram 

    actress Hayden, 25, looks radiant in the picture, just one month after giving birth to her first child. The engaged couple, who have been dating for five years, welcomed their daughter on 9 December.

    "She's really so sweet," Hayden told shortly afterwards. "I'm sure that's the way it's meant to be, because when they are putting you through the ringer you’re like, 'You are lucky you're so cute!'"

    The star added, "You're suddenly looking at this little thing, like, 'Oh, that was you in my belly this whole time.'"

    Hayden Panettiere and Wladimir Klitschko welcomed Kaya on 9 December

    Parenthood has been a magical experience for both Hayden and Wladimir, who has "always wanted to be a dad".

    "I think at some point he might gave started thinking he wasn't ever going to be (a father)," Hayden told the publication. "And then he was. He is just so over the moon."

    Wladimir himself recently spoke of his connection with his daughter. "You don't know what love is until you have your first baby, then you know what love is," he told .
