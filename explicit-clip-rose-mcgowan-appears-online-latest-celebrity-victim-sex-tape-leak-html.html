Rose McGowan becomes 'latest celebrity victim of sex tape leak'

Publish date: 2024-02-20

An X-rated clip of actress Rose McGowan has allegedly appeared online, making her the latest victim of a sex tape leak.

According to unconfirmed reports, just over two minutes of footage shows the 43-year-old with an unidentified male.

The video features on 'several celebrity websites' according to The Mirror, and allegedly involves Rose performing a sex act. 

Victim: Rose McGowan, pictured earlier this year,  has apparently been the subject of a sex tape leak online 

Rose has not yet commented on the reports, and MailOnline has reached out to her representatives.
