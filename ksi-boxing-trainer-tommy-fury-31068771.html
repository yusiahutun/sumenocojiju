Publish date: 2024-08-31

KSI’s former boxing trainer has claimed the YouTuber needs a “miracle” to beat Tommy Fury.

The two social media stars will go head-to-head at the Manchester Arena on October 14. Their highly-anticipated clash will see KSI’s Prime energy drink partner Logan Paul take on Dillon Danis on the undercard in a grudge match.

KSI, 30, currently boasts an unbeaten record across his three fights, though none have come against professional opponents. Meanwhile, Fury descends from a long line of boxing royalty and has nine professional wins under his belt.
