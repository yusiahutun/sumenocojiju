    From Michelle's enviable bikini posts, it might look like the glamorous star spends her life in the gym, but she doesn’t. Instead, she sets herself achievable and realistic targets to achieve her ultra-toned physique.

    Michelle star has a refreshing approach to fitness

    In an interview with , she revealed: "If I really, really don’t want to go to the gym – I don't go. I just don’t punish myself, it's not like, my life to go to the gym. If I can go to the gym I will, if I can't, I can't.
