Experience

Lauren has been in the editorial industry for over five years. Today, she is a contributing writer for InStyle where she covers a wide range of beauty topics including skincare, hair care, and makeup. Her passions include finding the best skin care products for every skin type, discovering new and innovative brands, and breaking down the latest trends. You can reach her on Instagram at @laurenharano and Twitter at @laurennharano.
