Music career

Anuel gained interest in music from a young age after seeing his father interact with acclaimed artists. At 14, he started recording music, and on reaching 18, he began posting it online. His music attracted millions of views, and eventually, he caught the attention of American rapper Rick Ross.

Rick Ross signed Anuel to the Latin division of Maybach Music Group. In February 2016, he released the mixtape Real Hasta la Muerte, and it received positive critic reviews. As a result, he landed a guest feature on Ozuna’s album Odisea in 2017.

The rapper attained a commercial breakthrough at a time no one would have expected. In April 2016, he was arrested in Puerto Rico and sentenced to 30 months in prison for illegally possessing three firearms. While incarcerated, he recorded his first album, titled like his debut mixtape.

The album was released on the day he was released after completing his sentence in 2018. It earned critical and commercial success, ranking 42 on the Billboard 200. It was also number one on the Top Latin Albums charts.
