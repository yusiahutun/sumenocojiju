Is he still married to Andrea Sarubbi?

Luke Farrell Kirby is an American-Canadian actor famous for his Lenny Bruce role in the Amazon Prime Video TV series The Marvellous Mrs. Maisel. He earned a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Guest Actor for the Lenny Bruce role.

Who is Andrea Sarubbi?

Luke Kirby's wife, Andrea Sarubbi, is a fashion designer. She is known for being part of the costume and wardrobe department of Treasure Seekers (1999).

Andrea has worked for the Irish Repertory Theatre as a costume designer and stage assistant for years. Her other projects include making costumes for California Costumes, Forum Novelties and Little Adventures.
