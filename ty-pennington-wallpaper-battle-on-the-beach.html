'Battle of the Beach' Star Ty Pennington Stuns Fan With &quot;Bold&quot; Suggestion

Publish date: 2024-04-27

Ty Pennington is known for his antics, regardless of what HGTV show he's on. But, dare we say, his latest stunt may take the cake as one of his funniest moves ever?

Last week, Pennington posted a clip from Battle On the Beach, where he serves as a mentor for a young couple as they compete against two other teams to get the most bang for their buck. Their goal is to make their previously identical home in Gulf Shores, AL, stand out against the other two and impress judges Bryan and Sarah Baeumler. Though Ty’s idea would definitely stand out, his team decided against, and we think you’ll see why.

“🚨 TOMORROW 🚨 Can’t think of a better way to spend Father’s Day than watching an all new episode of #BattleOfTheBuldge …. I mean, #BattleOnTheBeach 🤣 Catch it on @hgtv at 9/8c #dontmissit #lovemyteam,” Pennington captioned his reel.

Pennington's wallpaper features a photo of himself in a red speedo with a backwards hat. "I think it's subtle," he said of the wallpaper.

Though the Rock the Block host's team turned his wallpaper idea down, fans in the comments seemed to love the idea! "Now that's a wallpaper I can get behind!" his mentee, Ashley Basnight (@smashingdiy), replied.

Other fans joined in on the joke in the comments:

  • "Will this wallpaper pattern be available with your other product line in stores? Asking for a friend….😂❤️”
  • “😂 Yep, nothing subtle at all 😂😂. Only you Ty! 🙌😂❤️”
  • “I know @smashingdiy loves wallpaper, but whew, this one is BOLDDD 🤣”
  • "I think your mom would appreciate a bath room remodel with this paper as a surprise! 😂😂😂😂😂😂😂😂❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️”

If you missed last week's episode, you can always stream Battle on the Beach on Max, and don't forget to tune in for the three remaining episodes of season 3.
