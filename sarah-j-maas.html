Sarah J Maas – Awards and Nominations

Caption: Sarah J Maas at the 2015 Goodreads Choice of the Year Award ceremony (Source: Goodreads)

Sarah was nominated and awarded several times in her career. She received Best Young Adult Fantasy and Science Fiction for her popular books Queen of Shadows and A Court of Thorns and Roses. Similarly, she was awarded Best Young Adult Fantasy and Science Fiction for her book Kingdom of Ash in 2022. Maas received recognition from RT Book Reviews, including several RT Reviewers’ Choice Awards for her novels.

In 2016, the “A Court of Thorns and Roses” series was nominated for a Teen Choice Award in the “Choice Book: Fantasy” category. In 2016, Maas won the Compton Crook Award for Best First Novel for her book “Throne of Glass.” This award is presented by the Baltimore Science Fiction Society. She has been part of different awards and recognitions throughout the year.
