Kylie Jenner Celebrates 18th Birthday with Canadian Bash

Publish date: 2024-05-11

For Kylie Jenner, the party just don’t stop.

The youngest Keeping Up with the Kardashians star turned 18 on Aug. 10, and the birthday celebrations have been ongoing ever since. The latest stop: Montreal.

After a lavish bash in Los Angeles surrounded by her A-list friends and famous family, the reality star traveled to Punta Mita, Mexico, for an intimate vacation with her closest pals and her 25-year-old boyfriend, Tyga.

Following a quick pit stop back in Los Angeles, Jenner then jetted off to Canada on Sunday to host a party presented by InList at the Beachclub, an outdoor club 30 minutes from Montreal.

Jenner documented the day on her social media accounts, posting pictures and videos to Instagram and Snapchat.

Clad in a colorful long-sleeved mini dress with her ever-changing hair back to its natural color, the reality star hopped on board a chopper and then took a speedboat to the Beachclub, where she was greeted by hundreds of screaming fans.

Once at the venue, Jenner made a quick outfit change into a white two-piece skirt and crop top set and continued to party, sending her fans into a frenzy as she threw T-shirts into the crowd from the stage.

In one snap, the reality star – who is of the legal drinking age in Québec – displayed a customized champagne bottle with her name on it, captioning it “Legal.”

Jenner also posted a video from the party to her Instagram, captioning it “Snapchat poppin.”

“Brown lips,” she captioned a glamorous shot with her famous pout painted brown. “#KylieLipKit stay tuned.”
