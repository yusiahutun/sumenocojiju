Lee Brice and Wife Sara Welcome Baby Girl

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Lee Brice and his wife Sara have welcomed a baby girl into the family. Their first daughter was born on Friday morning (June 2).

"She's beautiful, happy and healthy," Brice's wife says.

Trulee Nanette Brice was born at 9AM on June 2 in Nashville, according to People. The baby girl weighed 7 lbs., 13 oz., and measured 20 inches at birth. She joins brothers Ryker (2013) and Takoda (2008).

"I’m so grateful to God for the health and blessing of Trulee in our lives,” Brice says, "And I’m so amazed by Sara. She was truly courageous through this special time, and beyond beautiful from the inside out."

The Cutest Baby Bumps in Country Music

Brice shared that he and Sara were expecting in January, later revealing that they'd be having a girl by letting their boys dig into cupcakes that shared the baby's gender. He also shared a video of the baby kicking inside Sara's belly in April. The "Boy" singer said baby girl was due this summer, but there's nothing to indicate there were any complications or that she was delivered early.

Brice's baby continues the country music baby boom of 2017. Brantley Gilbert, Thomas Rhett and Jason Aldean are three more country men expecting this year.
