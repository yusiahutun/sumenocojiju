Amir Odom - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-24

Content creator and conservative influencer known for videos reacting to liberal issues and "tough topics." He posted a video titled "Why white privilege isn't a big deal." He has amassed more than 130,000 subscribers on his Amir Odom YouTube channel.

Before Fame

He worked in marketing before joining YouTube in October 2019.

Trivia

He gained over 150,000 followers on Instagram in 2020 in large part by sharing his opinion on COVID and George Floyd. In one of his most viewed videos, he speaks about his concerns with the LGBTQ community. In another video, he talks about why Pride Month became the "most hated" month.

Family Life

He was born in Illinois before moving to Tennessee. He has a dog named Oliver. 

Associated With

In May 2023, he posted a video about Bud Light and Dylan Mulvaney. He has been good friends with fellow YouTube star Brett Cooper.
