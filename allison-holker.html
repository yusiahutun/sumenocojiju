Allison Holker Biography Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-02-12

Bio:
Allison Holker  (born on February 6, 1988 in Minnesota) is an American dancer and choreographer. While growing up in Utah, Allison began formal dance training when she was four years old and began teaching dance at age 16. She performed at the opening and closing ceremonies of the 2002 Olympic Games, before getting cast in High School Musical and High School Musical 2. She would go on to have multiple television appearances on shows like So You Think You Can Dance and the American version of The X Factor. She partnered with actor Johnathan Bennet on the 19th season of So You Think You Can Dance. She then partnered with Riker Lynch during Season 20, and Andy Grammer during Season 21.

Best Known For:
Allison Holker is best known as a professional dancer, having appeared on So You Think You Can Dance and Dancing With The Stars. 

Personal Life:
Allison gave birth to her daughter, Weslie Fowler, in 2008. In 2013, she married fellow So You Think You Can Dance all-star, Stephen “tWitch” Boss. On the Oct. 5 episode of DWTS, she announced they were expecting their first child together!
