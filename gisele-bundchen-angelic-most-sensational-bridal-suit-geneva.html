Gisele Bndchen is angelic in the most sensational leg-lengthening bridal suit

Publish date: 2024-06-07

The former Victoria's Secret Angel paired her white suit with the beachiest curls

    star teamed the blazer with a pair of wide-leg matching pants that skimmed the floor, revealing a glimpse of her gold strappy heels.
