The Kansas City Chief’s linebacker’s real name is Willie Gay Jr. He has no middle name.

As it is common in the United States for a person named William to be called Willie with love, people might think he has a different full name. However, it is not so.

On a different note, he is nicknamed Chip. His mom always calls him by nickname as she thought her child was as sweet as a chocolate chip.

Wille Gay Jr was born to his parents, Bridgett and Willie Gay Sr, on February 15, 1998, in Starkville, Mississippi. 

Although his parents had no athletic backgrounds, Willie developed an interest in sports at a young age. Growing up in Starkville, he attended Starkville High School, where he excelled in football.

An excellent overall defensive player, Willie led his team to the 2015 Class 6A state title win. Aside from garnering several honors throughout his time with Starkville, he became the institution’s first player to rush over 300 yards in a game in his senior season.
