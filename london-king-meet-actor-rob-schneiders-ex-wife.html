London King: Meet Actor Rob Schneider's Ex-Wife

Publish date: 2024-07-10

London King is a former model, actress, and TV personality who works as a childbirth educator and companion. However, she is famous as Elle King’s mother and the former wife of Rob Schneider. Schneider is an American actor, screenwriter, producer, and director who started his career performing stand-up comedy. However, he rose to fame as a cast member and writer for the NBC sketch comedy series “Saturday Night Live” from 1988-1994.

Following his departure from SNL, he pursued a career in feature films and starred in roles in several comedy films, including “The Hot Chick” (2002), “Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo” (1999), and “Big Stan” (2007). Conversely, his ex-wife, King, was never a mainstay in Hollywood’s elite roster. However, her disappearance from the acting scene has fans curious.

Here’s everything we know about her.

What Does London King, Rob Schneider’s Ex-Wife, Do for a Living?

King began her career as a model, appearing in numerous advertising campaigns. However, she failed in this field, as she did not get modeling offers from any famous brand. Her teenage pregnancy was also another factor that decelerated her modeling career. Hence, most of the gigs she got were small-scale ones. 

Due to her unsuccessful modeling career, Rob Schneider’s ex-wife decided to delve into a different field and tried her hands in the movie sphere. London King got her first big break after scoring the role of Cheryl in the 2000 film, “A Better Way to Die.” The following year, she appeared as Jade in the movie “Raw Fish” (2001) and followed that up with the 2002 film “The Calling,” where she took on the character Ruby Jenkins.

She is also renowned for being a pillar of support to women. In addition, King runs a website, “Push Love Doula,” where she blogs about childbirth, pregnancy, and child care.

Unfortunately, having a child together did not stop their marriage from being tumultuous. Rob Schneider and London King parted ways on September 1, 1990. While London King’s first child, Noah, stays away from the limelight, her second child, Elle King, grew up to become a renowned singer, songwriter, and actress. Her music blends various genres, including rock, blues, country, and soul.
