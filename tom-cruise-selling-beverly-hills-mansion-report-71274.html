REPORTTom Cruise is Selling His Beverly Hills Mansion for $50 Million

Publish date: 2024-06-27

Tom Cruise is moving on from his Beverly Hills home.

According to Variety, the 53-year-old actor has quietly put his California mansion on the market with an asking price of $50 million.

Tom and his then-wife, Katie Holmes — the couple split in 2012 and are parents to a nine-year-old daughter, Suri — reportedly bought the lavish house in 2007 for $30.5 million.

Tom’s Beverly Hills mansion, photographed in 2013.

According to public tax records obtained by Variety, the 10,000-square-foot adobe includes seven bedrooms and nine bathrooms and is situated on 1.3 acres of land. The property’s exterior additionally boasts an in-ground pool, tennis court, basketball court, children’s playground and several guesthouses.

This is just the latest in a slew of recent property sales for the Mission Impossible star — he sold his NYC apartment a few years ago for $3 million, earned $12 million in the sale of his LA compound and his Telluride, Colo. home is now on the market for a reported $59 million.
