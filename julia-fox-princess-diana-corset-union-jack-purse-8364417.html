Julia Fox Paid Homage to Princess Diana With a Corset Featuring the Royal's Face and a Union Jack Pu

Publish date: 2024-06-04

Julia Fox and her strikingly distinctive fashion have been also over the cobblestone streets of London this week, from her nothing but a dry-cleaning garment bag and a pair of white bloomers to her sexy Jack Skellington-inspired gown. But her latest London look reigns supreme and pays homage to the late Princess Diana.

On Thursday, Julia was photographed in a custom cream corset top by Eve Corsets that was printed with an image of the late princess’s face. Fox continued the royal punk vibes by pairing the corset with a white pleated lace midiskirt from Chopova Lowena and a massive coordinating, studded belt. The actress added a hint of Ginger Spice with a Union Jack handbag from Mowalola shoulder, and she finished off the look with white leather cowgirl boots, a smattering of silver bracelets, and a coordinating choker with her name “Julia."

She styled her auburn hair in a half-up, half-down hairstyle with face-framing bangs, and a giant white bow adorned the 'do. When it came time for glam, she complemented her glowing complexion with a generous swirl of blush, shimmery eyes, and glossy pink lips.

Last week, Fox released her new memoir, Down the Drain, and talked about her past drug use, including a near-death experience that she had when she overdosed.

"I always go back to that one instance of seeing that light and feeling so warm — almost too warm — and I just knew that I wasn't gonna come back if I went to that place," she said during an appearance on The View. "In a way, that happening really restored my faith."

She also shared with both the Los Angeles Times and Rolling Stone that she is taking Suboxone, a drug used to treat opioid addiction. She told the publications that the medication "saved my life, for sure."
