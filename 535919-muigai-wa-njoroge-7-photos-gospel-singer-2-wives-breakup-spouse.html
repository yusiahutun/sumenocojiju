Why Muigai married 2nd wife

During a recent interview with Kameme FM, Njeri said her husband kept mentioning getting a second wife during their first stages of marriage, an idea she was not okay with.

"In our small house, he told me. I would tell him that I would never accept that arrangement," she explained.

Nine months into their marriage, Muigai married Queen Stacey as his second wife, and Njeri said things changed. She was very bitter and cried for days.

"When he married her, I was so bitter. This was 12 years ago," she said.

The mother of four said she became an alcoholic, thinking it was a dream, but alcohol did not solve her problems.

Njeri and Stacey, best co-wives?

Njeri and Stacey were branded the best co-wives by netizens. The two mothers did everything together and were each other's pillar, but in mid-2023, netizens started noticing they were drifting apart.

The two no longer stopped hanging out together and posting each other on social media. In May last year, they stole the show at their husband's 25th anniversary celebration in the music industry.

Muigai wa Njoroge and Queen Stacey's wedding

On August 26, Muigai and his Stacey tied the knot after 12 years. The couple's traditional wedding was attended by several politicians and celebrities, among them Limuru MP John Kiragu, MCA Kung'u Smart and Henry Desagu. Philanthropist Muraya Karangu emceed the glamourous ceremony.
