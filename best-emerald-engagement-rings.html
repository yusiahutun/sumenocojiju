12 best emerald engagement rings - and their special meaning

Publish date: 2024-04-13

These gorgeous emerald engagement rings are perfect for brides who like to stand out

    Looking to propose to your loved one? Selecting an emerald engagement ring means opting for a special jewel that really stands out. They are not only utterly mesmerising, but emeralds are also practical too as the stone itself is rather scratch resistant.

    SHOP: 28 of the best diamond engagement rings from Vera Wang, Tiffany and more

    As well as symbolising true love, emerald gemstones have connotations of wealth and fortune. The green colour means that they represent the vibrancy of nature.

    Celebrities who own an emerald engagement ring

    Victoria Beckham owns a whopping 14 different engagement rings, and one of them is a huge cushion-cut emerald. Halle Berry is also part of the emerald ring club with a dazzling 4-carat green gem. If it's good enough for the A-list, it's good enough for us.
