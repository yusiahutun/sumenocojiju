Does Kylie Jenner Wear Glasses?

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Yesterday, Kylie Jenner took to her Instagram story to discuss the exciting launch of Kylie Cosmetics at Ulta Beauty. But, her viewers seemed to be a lot more interested in her surprising reading glasses. After a series of videos talking about her beauty empire, Jenner's story soon turned into a full Q&A about her frames.

"Also, just an announcement for everyone that's asking, yes these are real glasses," she said to the camera. "When I was younger I always wanted cute ass glasses. Always wanted glasses! Had the best vision though. Now that I have to wear glasses, this sh*t sucks."

Jenner continued, "And yes, they are Prada for the one girl that just asked on Twitter for anyone who wants them. These are definitely the ones that I get the most compliments on."

"Alright, well I didn't know that this Insta story time would turn..into talking about glasses," Kylie later added. "But since someone you guys keep asking, someone just said 'how many glasses do I have.' I had about four pairs, but these are the last ones standing because I wear my glasses to watch TV at night and I end up falling asleep, and squashing them all in the middle of the night."

We feel you, Kylie.
