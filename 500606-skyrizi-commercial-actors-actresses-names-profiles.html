1. Jane Treacy

Jane Treacy is one of the most disliked hosts on QVC. Many people have accused her of being condescending, rude to visitors, and making the audience uncomfortable. Some viewers complained that she had a habit of dismissing guests on air.

2. Shawn Killinger

Shawn is a familiar face on QVC, having worked there for over a decade. Regardless, she is considered an unpopular host on QVC for various reasons. Some people hate her personality, terming her as obnoxious and fake.

Other people don't appreciate her sense of humour as they don't find her funny. Some viewers think Killinger is not good at hosting as she appears scripted and uneasy when on air. Shawn is a lifestyle, beauty, fashion, and travel host on QVC.

3. Doris Dalton

Doris is one of the renowned QVC presenters thanks to her overall demeanour and raspy voice. Unfortunately, she is one of the disliked hosts, as many people find her pushy and downright obnoxious. At some point in her career, a viewer petitioned for her removal from QVC but was unsuccessful.
