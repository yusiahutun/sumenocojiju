Holly Willoughby's shirt soaked see-through in This Morning blunder

Publish date: 2024-09-23

The 37-year-old looked radiant on today's This Morning in powder blue shorts paired with the matching blazer.

Beneath her suit, Holly Willoughby sported a smart white shirt and minimal accessories.

However, This Morning's Holly Willoughby suffered an unfortunate mishap when she met Maddie Moate.

Holly chatted with the CBBC host alongside her co-star Phillip Schofield, 56, about the latest science crazes for children.

But disaster struck when Holly completed one task, which involved making snake bubbles using washing up liquid.

The blonde blew the concoction so much that it landed on her shirt and soaked it see-through right across her chest, wet t-shirt competition style.

"Oh no! I'm having a shirt issue," Holly cried in horror, which sent viewers into meltdown.

"I was screaming at the TV, 'Your shirt!'" one tweeted.

"Oh my goodness. Holly's outfit!" another added.

And third was only focused on her pins, writing: "Keep on flaunting those lovely legs of yours."

Although Holly looked amazing in her summery ensemble, that wasn't her only fashion mishap today.

Holly and Phil opened This Morning by arriving in the studio in an huge army tank ahead of Armed Forces Day on June 30.

But while Phil bounced out of the tank to present the show, Holly failed to make a graceful exit.

The mother-of-three struggled to clamber out of the huge vehicle, and cried: "It's really hard getting out of a tank in heels you know!"

This Morning airs weekdays on ITV at 10:30am.
