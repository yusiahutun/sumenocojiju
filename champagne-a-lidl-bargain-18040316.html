Champagne a Lidl bargain - Daily Star

Publish date: 2024-05-10

Fizzy tipples are usually deemed an expensive luxury treat.

But bargain-packed supermarket Lidl is wooing punters with high-class booze at rock-bottom prices.

A bottle of Chaumet Brut Premium Sparkling Perry costs just £1.

Lidl claims it’s the world’s cheapest bottle of bubbly. Public relations manager Marco Ivone said: “We are selling the bubbly for just £1 so everyone can enjoy a touch of luxury this Christmas.

“We’ve made what is normally considered an exclusive tipple affordable.

“It’s particularly important to offer value for money in the current economic times.”

Made in Liverpool, the sparkling perry is 5.5% volume.

At £1 for 75cl it costs a staggeringly cheap £1.33 per litre.

It gives shoppers far more for their money than traditional budget bevvies such as Special Brew lager at £3 per litre and Diamond White cider at £2.07.

Mr Ivone added: “It’s a world first and we are pleased to be bringing this to the British public.”
