Albanian presenter Greta Hoxha could be starring on Australian naked news channel

Publish date: 2024-06-14

A topless Albanian newsreader who was propelled to global fame for her scantily clad news flashes could soon have a crack at presenting fully naked.

Greta Hoxha, from the Albanian capital Tirana, was thrust into the spotlight after delivering the day's TV headlines clad in the skimpiest of waistcoats - and nothing underneath.

The 24-year-old could now be jetting to Australia after receiving an offer from a new channel where presenters deliver the bulletins stark naked.

The station has reportedly offered Ms Hoxha a salary worth nearly $4,800AUD per week and accommodation in Sydney.

A letter from the station boss Kimmy Dee laid the proposal bare: 'I've seen your video on 'YouTube'. Here in Australia, a new television station is opening and we will present news with naked news presenters.'
