B2K Officially Breaks Up

Publish date: 0001-01-01

(CelebrityAccess News Service) — The members of R&B/hip-hop group B2K have called it quits. B2K's Omarion, Lil Fizz, J-Boog and Raz have broken up after six years together. Rumors have been speculating a breakup inside the B2K camp for some time now, and disagreements amongst the quartet are the reason behind the band's breakup.

"We've had a great run together making hit songs, albums and now movies," said longtime manager and production partner Chris Stokes of the Ultimate Group. "The kids are growing up and are interested in pursuing their own careers. I have enormous respect for each of them, and I wish them success in their pursuit of their individual careers. B2K was an extremely talented singing group that will surely be missed." –Bob Grossweiner and Jane Cohen
