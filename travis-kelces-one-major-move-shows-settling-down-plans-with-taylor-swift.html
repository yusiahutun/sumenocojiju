Travis Kelce's one major move shows 'settling down' plans with Taylor Swift

Publish date: 2024-05-21

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are moving too swiftly and sources share one gesture of the NFL heartthrob that shows he is ready to make a big decision with the singer-songwriter.

Gone are the days when Taylor Swift‘s relationship details were only to be guessed by her lyrics and wardrobe hints. The music legend is welcoming a new era by being more open about her love life. She even opened up about what changed to make her embrace a public relationship with Travis Kelce.

Given all the clues, fans speculate Tay Tay and Travis might be ready for the next big step.

Travis Kelce bought a new house thinking of Taylor Swift

Fans were fairly surprised to learn that Travis Kelce bought a $6 million mansion in Kansas City right after he started dating Taylor Swift. A source close to the celebrity reveals that his major move was not an impulsive decision but more of a sincere gesture for the music icon.

Speaking of Travis’s feelings, the insider offered: “Travis bought the house with Taylor in mind. He wants to show Taylor he’s the settling-down type, and he wants her to feel comfortable there.”

The insider also revealed that the NFL star did not forget to show the place to Taylor before making the final decision. “He paid for the house, but he’s making Taylor feel like it’s her home, too.”

Travis is ‘happy’ taking Taylor Swift’s interior design output

From writing Grammy-winning song lyrics to picking out aesthetic furniture for her beau, Taylor offers a finesse in every new venture that she dabbles in. People close to the singer say that she has put on her interior designer hat for boyfriend Travis.

Since Travis has bought the new house, Taylor has picked out furniture and decor for the new place to make it more homely. “Travis welcomes her input,” the source disclosed.

Recently, moving vans have been spotted next to Trav’s mansion, and reports claim that the celebrity couple is planning to spend their holidays together.

Before Taylor’s 2024 leg of Eras tour begins, the 34-year-old billionaire will be resting for a while and rumors say that she might just move in with the Catching Kelce star for a while.
