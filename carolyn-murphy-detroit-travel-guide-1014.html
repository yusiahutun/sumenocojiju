Carolyn Murphy Shinola - Detroit Travel Guide

Publish date: 2024-04-21

Media Platforms Design Team

The model turned women's design director of the Detroit heritage brand Shinola lets us in on her Motor City hot spots. "I stay at this cool B&B called Honor & Folly, and stop by Willys Detroit for a Drought juice—it's one of the best places in the country to shop. And Eastern Market, Antiques Mall, and Shinola are standouts. Head to Bronx Bar for a beer and Raven Lounge for late-night fun. You've gotta have a crazy hot dog called a Coney from American Coney Island, and Slows Bar BQ is fantastic. Check out Dabl's African Bead Gallery, the Heidelberg Project's decorated houses, and the Detroit Institute of Arts."
