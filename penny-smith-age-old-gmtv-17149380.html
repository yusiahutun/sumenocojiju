Penny Smith, 60, champions cleavage in perilously plunging gown

Publish date: 2024-02-26

The Wright Stuff presenter has been on TV screens for 25 years.

Penny Smith, who started her career on GMTV, has gained a firm fanbase during her time in the spotloight.

Now 60-year's-old, Penny proved last night she's the ultimate ageless beauty.

Penny certainly made sure all eyes were on her as she headed to the Costa Book of the Year Awards.

The blonde bombshell looked every inch the glamourpuss as she wore a sparkling purple gown, covered in glitter.

She paired the show-stopping number with blush-pink strappy heels and shiny skin-coloured tights.

Penny pulled her hair back into a messy up-do, which saw loose tendrils fall around her face.

She kept her makeup understated, opting for a dewy glow foundation and a pale pink gloss.

Last year, Penny had her first taste of reality TV, when she apeared on Channel 5's Celebrity Go Barging.

Speaking to the Express, Penny revealed that the experience that saw her travel the River Lot and the Canal du Midi, wasn’t as easy as she thought it would be.

She said: “I did my Powerboat Stage One test on a tiny little boat on the Isle of Wight, and thought the chance to tackle a bigger boat would be great.”

Penny continued: “Even though I’d driven a boat just twice to get my powerboat licence, I was moderately confident and thought I was quite good.

“However, when you watch the first episode you see me proved catastrophically wrong!”
