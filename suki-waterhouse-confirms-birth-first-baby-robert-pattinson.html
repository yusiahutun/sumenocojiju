Suki Waterhouse Confirms Birth of Her and Robert Pattinson's Baby

Publish date: 2024-07-03

Suki Waterhouse is a mom!

The English actor and model just confirmed she and Robert Pattinson have welcomed their first child together. She shared the exciting news in an Instagram post late on Thursday night, with a Polaroid of herself holding her newborn. In the caption, Waterhouse wrote, “Welcome to the world angel ❤️.”

In the photo, the Daisy Jones & the Six star wears a pewter-gray knitted cardigan over a dark undershirt, and she cradles her baby in her arms. The newborn (whose name has not yet been revealed) is seen swaddled in a blanket adorned with blue and pink hearts.

Waterhouse’s post arrives a little over a week after the Daily Mail captured photos of her and Pattinson strolling through Los Angeles with a stroller, seemingly confirming the arrival of their baby. In those photos, Waterhouse wore a white baseball cap and a black trench, which she layered over a periwinkle jumper and sweats. Pattinson wore a gray hoodie under a foggy-green puffer, along with black shorts and a black cap. The pair appeared to be keeping under the radar as they took their walk.

The actors have been linked since the summer of 2018, and Pattinson first acknowledged their relationship a year later, when he told The Sunday Times they were trying to keep their romance private. “If you let people in, it devalues what love is,” he said. “If a stranger on the street asked you about your relationship, you’d think it extremely rude. If you put up a wall, it ends better.”

Just this past fall, Waterhouse gave rare insight into their relationship during a visit to the Apple podcast Driven Minds. While talking about moving into the same space with Pattinson, she told host Gillian Sagansky, “I’ve been having such a crushing weight of anxiety because I’ve moved. I wasn’t there for the move, so everything just got boxed up and sent.” Waterhouse added that she’d hired a professional cleaner to help organize her “collection of mess.”

But, she said, she was happy to have the Twilight alum there to greet her at the door. “I’m very lucky because he is so accepting of the mess and the chaos,” she said. “He never says anything about it. He kind of thinks it’s like, charming. So I’m very lucky for that.”

Waterhouse first revealed her pregnancy in November, when she showed off her baby bump at the Corona Capital 2023 Music Festival in Mexico City. “I thought I’d wear something sparkly to distract you from something else I’ve got going on,” she said while cradling her belly. “I’m not sure it’s working.”
