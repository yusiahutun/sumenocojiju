What is the most attractive hair/eye combo for men and women?

Publish date: 2024-04-24

Different human beings have different hair and eye colour combinations. Hereditary genes determine these combinations. So, what are the most attractive hair/eye combo for men and women?

Melanin is a pigment that gives colour to your eyes, hair, and skin. Whereas genes determine your eye colour, the amount of melanin determines the colour of your hair. Here are some of the most attractive hair and eye colour combinations you will come across.

Most attractive hair/eye combo

What is the most attractive hair eye combo for male or female people? Beauty lies in the eye of the beholder. However, there is a wide range of hair and eye colour combinations you will love. Some are hard to come by, and others are common.

1. Red hair and blue eyes

Red hair and blue eyes are widespread in Western European countries, including Norway, Sweden, Germany, and England. The beauty of this fantastic combination is quickly recognisable. This hair-eye combination on women tends to be very warm, soft, and alluring to most men.

2. White hair and blue eyes

This hair colour with blue eyes takes your beauty to a new level. This is an uncommon combination, but there are quite a few exciting things about it. A majority of people with this combination are from the European region.

3. Brown hair and green eyes

Brown hair is the most common hair colour. However, it is rare to find a combination with green eyes. Many people with green eyes tend to try out this hair combination if they do not have it naturally. Warm undertones of a chestnut brown hair colour will help make your green eyes pop.

4. Brown hair and light hazel eyes

A darker hair colour makes the eyes look bold and bright. A deep brown hair colour brings out the green and gold flecks in hazel eyes, creating a stunning combination. It is considered by many to be the best hair and eye colour combination.

5. Red hair and green eyes

Red hair and green eyes are not exactly a combination you would encounter daily. It is considered quite uncommon. However, this eye colour is captivating, especially on a face with good-looking features.

6. Black hair and purple eyes

According to MedicineNet, purple or violet eyes can naturally occur due to a mutation, inflammation inside the eye, or a condition called albinism. This eye and hair colour combination stands out for its mesmerising appearance that exudes an aura of mystery and elegance.

7. Red hair and dark blue eyes

Red hair and blue eye traits are also uncommon worldwide, and you could think it would be the rarest combination. The majority of redheads have brown, hazel, or green eyes. Red hair can range from strawberry blonde to the deepest auburn.

8. Black hair and green eyes

It is relatively rare for someone to have naturally black hair and green eyes. While black hair is one of the most common natural hair colours worldwide, green eyes are one of the rare eye colours. Only a tiny portion of the population worldwide has this combination.

9. Blonde hair and dark brown eyes

People with blonde hair and brown eyes are not very common, but not as rare as you think. This combination is known as blonde-brown. It is found in about 1 to two percent of the world's population.

This combination could be the most attractive hair eye combo for guys. Blonde hair paired with brown eyes can create a striking, beautiful look. People without this combination but with dark brown eyes can bleach their hair to attain the fantastic mix.

10. Black hair and black eyes

Black eyes always stand out, whether your skin is light or dark. They are shiny and intense. Dark hair also looks more glossy compared to other colours. This is the most popular hair and eye colour combination in the African population.

However, the range of skin colours associated with black hair is vast. They range from the palest of light skin tones to dark skin.

What is the relationship between hair colour and eye colour?

The relationship between hair colour and eye colour is melanin pigmentation.

Melanin is a naturally occurring substance or pigment produced by special skin cells called melanocytes. The cells are found in the skin, hair follicles, eyes and other body parts.
