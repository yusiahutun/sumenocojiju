Meet Cristina Invernizzi, Jordan Belfort's Wife

Publish date: 2024-06-21

Cristina Invernizzi is most famous as the wife of Jordan Belfort, known as the original “Wolf of Wall Street.” Invernizzi is an actress and model. She tied the knot with Belfort in October 2021, becoming his third wife. 

Belfort rose to fame because of his book, “The Wolf of Wall Street.” He became even more popular after its big-screen adaptation was released. It was also named “The Wolf of Wall Street.” It starred the award-winning actor Leonardo DiCaprio, who played Belfort in the movie.

Thanks to his book, a lot is known about Belfort but what about his third wife? Keep reading to learn more about Cristina Invernizzi. 

Who Is Cristina Invernizzi?

Cristina is a model who has competed in multiple beauty pageants and has won some, including the excellence exhibition Punta del Este in Uruguay. She was on vacation in the country when she got in and won the pageant.

She is most famous as Belfort’s wife, but she also has a successful career and is doing well. The couple dated for two years before they tied the knot in February 2021 in Las Vegas. 

Invernizzi and Belfort kept their marriage under wraps for months before Belfort announced the good news at an October charity event. She and the businessman first met in a bar in Guadalajara, a city in western Mexico. Belfort had been in Mexico to give a speech at an event while Invernizzi was in the country to pursue her acting career.

She and her husband are often seen on vacations and spending time in each other’s company. Belfort, who usually posts about his wife on social media, has described Cristina as the woman who made him want to be the best version of himself. 

On July 27, 2021, he took to his Instagram page to post a photo that captured him and Invernizzi on a plane. In the adorable picture, she can be seen stretching her hand across the aisle for Belfort to kiss. 

He captioned the post: “Traveling with my favorite co-pilot.” For Father’s Day, he shared a photo of himself and his wife with his children.

Cristina Invernizzi Bio

According to some sources, Invernizzi was born in Arlington, Texas, sometime in the early 1990s. Others claim she was born in Argentina. However, her exact birth details have not been made public. 

Some have claimed her ethnicity is Spanish. However, she is more likely to be Italian. Cristina Invernizzi studied human resource management after graduating high school. Before she became a full-time pro model, she was an auctioneer. In addition, she has reportedly worked as a public broker like Jordan Belfort.

What Is Cristina Invernizzi’s Net Worth? 

Invernizzi has a net worth of about $5 million. She reportedly lives a luxurious lifestyle thanks mostly to her thriving modeling career. On the other hand, her husband, Jordan Belfort, is worth about $100 million. 

Cristina Invernizzi Height

Invernizzi stands at a portable 5 feet 6 inches and weighs about 56 kg. She has black-colored eyes and luscious brown hair. She is fair in complexion and is blessed with a fantastic voluptuous body figure. 

Cristina Invernizzi and Jordan Belfort’s Children 

At the time of this writing, Jordan Belfort is known to have two children, Chandler and Carter. They are products of his marriage to his second wife, Nadine Caridi. He does not have kids with any other woman, including his first wife, Denise Lombardo. 

The former couple were childhood sweethearts. They married in 1985 and decided to go their separate ways in 1991. It was later that year he married Caridi. 

Cristina Invernizzi Birthday

The beautiful model was reportedly born on April 3, 1990, so she celebrates her birthday every April. 

Cristina Invernizzi Age

Invernizzi entered this world on April 3, 1990. As such, her age is 33 as of 2023. Her husband is 30 years older than her. 

She reportedly holds American nationality and is of mixed ethnicity. Some sources claim her father is an entrepreneur in the United States, while her mother was a house helper. She has a sister named Ornella Invernizzi. She attended a local private high school and went to an undisclosed college.
