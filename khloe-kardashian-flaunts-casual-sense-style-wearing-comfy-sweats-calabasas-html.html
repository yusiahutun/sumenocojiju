Khloe Kardashian flaunts her casual sense of style wearing comfy sweats out in Calabasas

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Khloe Kardashian opted for stylish comfort wearing fashionable sweats as she left a studio in Calabasas on Wednesday.

The Good America founder, 38, easily balanced not just one, but two large bags over her arm to hold an assortment of items she needed during her busy day.

The media personality, whose ex Tristan Thompson was recently seen embracing a mystery woman, glammed up her look that comprised of a tan-colored hooded sweatshirt and matching sweatpants.

The mother of two slipped into a pair of brown and white sneakers to coincide with the overall color scheme of her outfit.

Her long locks were parted to the side, and effortlessly flowed down past her shoulders in light, bouncy curls.

The TV personality threw on a pair of large, reflective shades to protect her eyes in case the bright sun peaked through the overcast sky. 

The businesswoman lugged a pink, Christian Dior tote bag along with a light blue Hermes Birkin bag over her left arm. 

She added a nude-colored matte tint to her lips, while the rest of her makeup was partially concealed by her stylish sunglasses. 

Khloe recently opened up about her fashion choices and pieces that she tends to wear, aside from stylish sweats. 

During a joint interview with Kris Jenner for Marie Claire earlier last month, the mother of two expressed, 'I love a jumpsuit. I do. I'm a jumpsuit girl.' 

'I think a jumpsuit is more of a night-time thing, but you could definitely wear it in the daytime. You feel sexy, and it's a no-brainer. You don't have to think about the rest of the outfit.' 

When describing if she has a 'signature' style, the media personality stated, 'For me, I would say sexy. I like to be a little edgy...' 

When talking to Bustle last year, she also gushed, 'I love denim. I do feel more sexy when I’m in something bodycon and just, like, something really fit and snug. . Even at my biggest, I was always someone who liked to be in tight clothes.'
