See Jennifer Garners Sweet Father's Day Tribute to Ben Affleck

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Jennifer Garner kept her ex-husband in mind while posting a Father’s Day tribute on social media this past weekend.

On Sunday, the 13 Going on 30 star shared a photo of herself with her parents, William and Patricia Garner. In the sweet snap, she wears a relaxed outfit consisting of a white T-shirt, a beige cardigan, faded blue jeans, white sneakers, and thick black rim glasses. “Happy Father’s Day to the best to ever do the job,” she wrote in the caption. She also acknowledged her fashion faux pas, adding, “(Jeans in church 😬— I made my connecting flight, but my luggage didn’t 🙏). We love you, Dad. X”

At the end of the note, Garner also nodded to Ben Affleck, with whom she shares three kids: 17-year-old daughter Violet, 13-year-old daughter Seraphina, and 10-year-old son Samuel.

“PS Shout out to BGA,” the actor wrote, using Affleck’s initials (his middle name is Géza). “No one loves their kids like you love ours, happy Father’s Day, Ben! X”

Garner and Affleck were married from 2005 to 2018. After their divorce, Affleck went on to marry Jennifer Lopez in a lavish ceremony held in Georgia last year, while Garner has been romantically linked to longtime boyfriend John Miller.

Affleck previously opened up about the end of his marriage to Garner in a 2021 interview with Howard Stern.

“The truth was, we took our time, we made the decision … We grew apart,” he said at the time. “We had a marriage that didn’t work, this happens, with somebody that I love and respect, but to whom I shouldn’t be married any longer. Ultimately, we tried. We tried, we tried because we had kids. Both of us felt like we don’t want this to be the model that our kids see of marriage.”
