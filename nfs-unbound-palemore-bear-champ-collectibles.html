Palemore Bear Champ Locations - Need For Speed Unbound

Publish date: 2024-03-17

Need For Speed Unbound Palemore Bear Champs are Collectibles that can grant you some extra cash for each one you get. The same goes for the 8 Street Art and 3 Billboards, also obtainable in the Palemore district or area. However, the Palemore Bear Champs can be tricky to find if you don’t know where to look, especially one of them, which will require a stunt on your part to get to it.

NFS Unbound Palemore Bear Champ Locations Map

Before going after each collectible, check the NFS 2022 map below to learn the exact locations of the Bears you must find.

Now, here is how to get them in the same order as the one on the map.

Bear Champ #1: Near Some Scaffolds

The first Bear Champ is found on the east side of the Palemore district (check the map above). More exactly between two buildings near the scaffolds, as shown in the next image.

Bear Champ #2: On the Railway

The second collectible is a little tricky because it’s the one that requires the stunt we were talking about earlier. First, head to the middle of the Palemore area at the location marked with “A” on our map. Once you reach the said location, look for the ramp leading to the railway above.

Now, go back a couple of meters to make sure you can gain enough speed so you can make the jump from the ramp onto the railway. Then proceed on the railway, avoiding any train that may pass, and you should soon reach the second Palemore Bear Champ.

Bear Champ #3: Near Some Dumpsters

The last collectible is a short ride north of the previous one, so get off the railway and head to the third location marked on the map above. You’ll find the Bear Champ near some dumpsters in a pedestrian area between two sets of stairs.
