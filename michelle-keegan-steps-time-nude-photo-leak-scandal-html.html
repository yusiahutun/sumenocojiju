Michelle Keegan steps out for the first time since nude photo leak scandal

Publish date: 2024-09-30

Michelle Keegan was this week named as one of the British celebrities on a list of 101 whose nude personal photos had been leaked on the internet.

But the actress was doing well to prove she'd put the ordeal behind her with a wide smile as she was lead through London on the arm of supportive boyfriend Mark Wright on Monday night.

The handsome couple put a brave face on a tough week, stepping out for a romantic dinner at Zuma restaurant.

Brave faces: Michelle Keegan looked pleased to be back on the arm of beau Mark Wright as she put a smile on in the face of reports nude photos of her had been leaked on the internet this week

Michelle, 27, walked behind her man Mark, also 27, as he clutched her hand tightly in a show of support for the actress.

The pair wore cosy, co-ordinating outfits on date night, with him sporting a cream knit and her choosing a chic over-sized cardigan coat.

Michelle picked a neutral palette matching tanned ankle boots to a leather bag and an Aztec cape, which fellow former Coronation Street actress Helen Flanagan was also wearing at the weekend.

Soap star intuition: Michelle stepped out in the same cape coat as her former Coronation Street co-star Helen Flanagan on Monday night

Hiding her flawless figure under the oversized cover up, actress-turned-fashion-designer Michelle dressed in an autumnal look accessorising with a grey woollen fedora over her shiny brunette tresses.

She flashed a wide smile for the cameras and shared only a hint of her famous frame in a pair of ripped jeans that highlighted her slim pins.

On Monday, Michelle's people released a statement that read: ‘There’s no evidence that Michelle’s phone or computer has been hacked and nothing has appeared online to suggest she has either.’

Teenage Disney stars Ariana Grande and Victoria Justice are among the US stars whose spokespeople have denied the authenticity of the photo leak.  

United front: Walking along with interlocked fingers, the couple shared bright smiles as they left a London restaurant on Monday

With the drama behind them, Mark and Michelle who are engaged to be married after meeting in Dubai over New Year, were clearly still loved up as they walked together with interlocking fingers .

On Sunday night, former TOWIE star Mark tweeted a sultry picture from his bed, saying how much he missed his brunette beauty.

He wrote 'When my girl ain't our bed it's a very lonely feeling !! @michkeegan hurry home Xx.' 

Loved up: Mark shared a sweet message for his brunette beauty on Sunday night when he said he was missing her from his empty bed 

Meanwhile, cheeky chap Mark is gearing up for this year's Strictly Come Dancing, telling The Mirror that it was an offer he just couldn't refuse.

'I just think it’s something you can’t turn down. It’s an incredible show, the best show on telly.

I’ve never danced before, learned anything like this in my life – it’s a huge challenge and if I can conquer it then I’ll be very happy.'

Mark can already boast an impressive physique, proving that the dancer's lifts might not be a problem for the star, but he did admit on his Heart radio show that his nickname was 'Marky-no-moves' from friends.
