First Woman to Win Best Msica Urbana Album at the Grammys

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Karol G solidified her spot as a leading Latina of music at the 2024 Grammys.

The Colombian superstar made history as the first woman to win Best Música Urbana Album, for 2023’s hit Mañana Será Bonito. The “Oki Doki” singer accepted her Grammy in a gorgeous silver gown (and pink hair!), thanking her fans, her team, and of course, her home country for supporting her.

“Hi, my name is Karol G, and I am from Medellín, Colombia,” she began her speech. “This is my first time at the Grammys, and this is my first time holding my own Grammy. I’m super happy, I’m super nervous, and I’m super excited to be in front of so many legends that I admire and respect. My album has given me the best memories of my whole life. My fans give me motivation and inspiration … I promise to give you my best always! I hope this is the first of so many.”

Christopher Polk//Getty Images

The singer is only the second artist ever to take home an award for Best Música Urbana Album. Since the category’s creation two years ago, Puerto Rican star Bad Bunny has ruled the category, winning in both 2022 and 2023.

Karol G spoke to Harper’s Bazaar in February 2023 about her now-award-winning album, and shared the significance of the record’s title, which translates to “Tomorrow Will Be Beautiful” in English.

“It means that it does not matter what happens today, because everything is going to be okay,” she said. “Sometimes we go through hard things, and we are just punching the air, like, ‘Why is this happening to me?’ The idea of the title is to embrace the moment.”
