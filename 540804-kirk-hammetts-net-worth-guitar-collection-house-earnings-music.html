Who is Kirk Hammett?

Kirk Hammett was born on 28 November 1962 in San Francisco, California, USA, to Teofila Oyao and Dennis L. Hammett, a Merchant Mariner. His mother was of Filipino descent, and his father was of English, German, Scottish, and Irish descent.

The popular guitarist was raised in El Sobrante, California, alongside his older brother, Rick Hammett. He attended De Anza High School in Richmond, California.
