Taylor Swift finally steps out in natural hair as fans beg 'never use straighteners again'

Publish date: 2024-02-25

Taylor Swift is letting her natural curls out in Australia, and fans are obsessed. The singer has taken her Eras Tour down under, and it going au naturel in the heat, as she steps out for dinner with Sabrina Carpenter.

The music star kicked off the record-breaking world tour last year and began the 2024 leg at the start of this month in Tokyo. She then quickly jetted back off to the States to see Travis Kelce and the Kansas City Chiefs take home the Super Bowl. Now, Taylor Swift is Down Under and her hair is making fans fangirl harder than ever.

Taylor Swift fans tell her to stick to her natural curls

The Blank Space singer was seen stepping out in Sydney with the Feather singer, as she embraced her natural hair, letting her curls hang loose.

Tay even went more natural with her make-up, dropping her signature red lip and opting for a nude look, as well as subtle glam.

Her outfit also slayed, as she paired a simple but chic black corset with a brown suede skirt with a black belt, and finished the look off with a gold necklace.

Fans are going wild for the look on X, formerly known as Twitter, as one exclaimed: “TAYLOR ALISON SWIFT PLS NEVER PICK UP A HAIR STRAIGHTENER AGAIN.”

“HER HAIR, HER OUTFIT. HER,” commented another.

A third wrote: “Unpopular opinion: Taylor Swift’s naturally wavy curly hair is way more beautiful than the straight hair she has had for years now!!!!!!”

“Things Taylor Swift makes me want: bangs and curly hair,” wrote another. Us too!

Travis Kelce may join his girlfriend in Australia

As the Football season draws to a close, fans are hoping Kelce flies out to Australia to join his girlfriend, and rumors are spreading that they’ll spend “a romantic holiday” in Queensland.

The Super Bowl winner dropped the hint on his New Heights Podcast with his brother, Jason Kelce, as he told him he would “venture to an island real soon.”

Of course, this sent fans into overdrive, especially when he said the best ones are ‘South of the US.’ From the looks of it, it looks like we could be seeing more Traylor content sooner than we thought!
