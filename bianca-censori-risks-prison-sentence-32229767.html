Bianca Censori risks prison sentence over striding out nearly naked in Paris

Publish date: 2024-02-22

Bianca Censori could face a prison sentence or a hefty fine after striding out nearly naked in Paris with her husband Kanye West.

The architect, 29, left her entire groin and bum exposed in a pair of sheer tights, slipping into a cropped blue top that appeared completely see-through and left her nipples on display. That outfit was followed by Bianca adding a fur coat with text stencilled into the back worn over her tights.

The pair were staying at the Ritz Hotel together, but Bianca didn’t seem to be aware that her antics could breach French decency laws – and potentially land her in a cell. Article 222-32 of the penal code from March 1994 states: “Deliberate sexual display in the sight of others in a place accessible to the public gaze is punishable by one year in prison and a fine of one hundred thousand francs.”

That’s roughly €15,000, or £13,000. But nudity itself isn’t equivalent to indecency, and the law further adds: “The charge has been worded in such a way as to rule out any possibility of proceedings against persons practising naturism in places specially designated for this purpose.”

The couple previously risked a hefty fine while strolling “virtually naked” around Italy. They faced paying €10,000 or imprisonment for going against the Italian Constitutional Court, which stipulates that a person who exposes themselves in or close to a place attended by minors could face anywhere from four months to four years imprisonment.

They were investigated by police when they were caught in a compromising position on a boat – with Kanye clad head to toe in black, including a hoodie and face mask, while Bianca wore next to nothing.

In Dubai, where modesty is advised in public places, Bianca was spotted in a tiny strapless white dress and clutching a huge teddy bear to cover her boobs as she went braless in the lobby of a hotel. Though there’s no official dress code in the country, tourists are advised to wear clothes that cover both shoulders and knees, and to avoid anything too tight – and Bianca was breaking all three unspoken rules.
