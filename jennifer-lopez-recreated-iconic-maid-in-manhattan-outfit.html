Jennifer Lopez recreates her iconic Maid in Manhattan outfit to promote Shotgun Wedding

Publish date: 2024-02-21

She levelled up the all-white ensemble

    Since April last year, an all-white sartorial concoction seems to be high up on Jennifer Lopez’s fashion schedule. Perhaps it’s because from the moment Ben Affleck proposed, she’s not ready to move out of her wedding bliss era? Or it could be because she plays a bride-to-be in so many movies that she’s subconsciously always in character.

    JLo wore Michael Kors to promote

    Either way, it’s a pleasure to watch. And to promote her upcoming film (guess what her character role is?) she wore a Marisa Ventura approved crisp white outfit that reminded us of her iconic outfit from 20 years ago.

    She looked magical in Michael Kors wearing a linen white shirt dress that was fastened only at the chest area. Thankfully she layered up for the winter (and did so excellently), wearing dazzling white palazzo pants underneath and a blazer over the top. A pair of platform gold heels and a gold belt on her trousers broke up the single shade, and gave mega celestial vibes.
