Im Buying These 8 Deals From Nordstroms Early Black Friday Sale

Publish date: 2024-08-09

As a shopping editor who’s obsessed with all things Nordstrom, I’m religiously refreshing the site for new arrivals from my favorite brands. But as fall settles in and the holidays quickly approach, I'm preparing my closet for colder weather — and my wallet for a lot of seasonal shopping. So, to reduce some of my spending on cold weather essentials and holiday gifts, I’ll be loading up my cart during Nordstrom's Fall Sale, aka its early Black Friday sale, which you can shop today through November 6.

Below, I rounded up eight fashion and beauty deals I plan on shopping, all from coveted brands like Levi’s, Free People, Yves Saint Laurent, and more, and prices start at $6.

Shop My On-Sale Nordstrom Fashion and Beauty Picks:

Nordstrom Lafayette Penny Loafer

I’m always on the lookout for a quality loafer, but even more so now that fall is here. Only recently, I’ve realized that I’m particularly drawn to penny loafers. So it was love at first sight when I came across Nordstrom’s gorgeous burgundy Lafayette Penny Loafers — and even better, they’re 55 percent off.

The slip-on shoe comes in black, white, tan, and burgundy, plus sizes 5 through 12. It features a smooth faux leather exterior, cushion insole for all-day comfort, and a rubber outsole with a half-inch heel that offers a good grip and arch support. 

Levi’s Faux Leather Racer Jacket

I’ve been wearing the same leather jacket for nearly a decade, and when I recently pulled it out of storage, it was in pretty bad shape. I’ve been looking for a more modern version to replace it, and I’ve had my eye on this Faux Leather Racer Jacket from Levi’s for a while, but it’s finally on sale right now. 

The motocross-style jacket comes in eight colors, including black, green, and pink, and sizes XS through XXL. It has a faux leather exterior and a full interior lining for warmth. The silhouette is a classic slim-straight fit with a zipper closure, two side zipper pockets, and decorative zipper detailing at the hem. The jacket also includes a functional buckle that fastens around the neck.

Yves Saint Laurent Touche Éclat All-Over Brightening Concealer Pen

Yves Saint Laurent’s Touche Éclat All-Over Brightening Concealer Pen has been a game changer for my minimal makeup routine, and now that I am starting to lose my summer tan, I need to stock up on a lighter shade while it’s 15 percent off.

This light-coverage concealer comes in 11 shades and is designed to brighten, highlight, and smooth your complexion. To apply, click the button at the bottom of the pen to push the product up into a tapered brush tip. The formula includes hyaluronic acid and vitamin E, both of which add hydration and fight signs of aging. I use it under my eyes to conceal dark circles and on the bridge of my nose and peaks of my brow bone as a highlighter.
