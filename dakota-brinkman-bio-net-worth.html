Dakota Brinkman Bio: Facts about American Actor and Celebrity Son

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Dakota Brinkman is an American actor, known for his appearance in the movie Laws of Deception (1997) who came to the media limelight as the son of the famous American actress and director, Melissa Gilbert.

Where and When was Dakota Brinkman born?

Dakota Brinkman opened his eyes as Dakota Paul Brinkman on 1st May 1989, in New York City, New York, the United States of America. As of 2022, his age is 30 years old while his horoscope is Taurus. He is the son of Melissa Gilbert (mother) and Bo Brinkman (father). Further, his grandparents are Kathy Wood and David Darlington.

He grew up with his three half-brothers Michael, Sam, and Lee Boxleitner. His half-brother, Michael, and Sam are actors while his other half-brother, Lee is a film director. Dakota holds an American nationality and belongs to white ethnical background.

Who is Dakota Brinkman’s Mother?

His mother, Melissa Gilbert is an American actress as well as a director who is widely famous for the television show Little House on the Prairie (1974-1983). Further, she is also recognized as The Queen of television movies and mini-series cause she has appeared in more than 45 TV shows.

Relationship Status

Awards

Little House on the Prairie

Little House on the Prairie is one of the famous American television series. It was premiered on the NBC network on 11th September 1974 while the last episode was aired on 10th May 1982.

The series is all about a family living on a farm in Walnut Grove, Minnesota, in the 1870s and 1880s. The series stars Michael Landon, Melissa Gilbert, and Karen Grassle as the lead role.

Laws of Deception

Laws of Deception is an erotic suspense thriller movie that was produced in the year 1997. The movie was written by Rollin Jarrett while it was directed by Joey Travolta. Further, the movie stars C Thomas Howell, James Russo, Amber Smith, Nick Mancuso, and Brian Austin Green in a lead roles.
