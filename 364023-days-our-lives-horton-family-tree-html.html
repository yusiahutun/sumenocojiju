Days of Our Lives Horton family tree

The Horton lineage was traditionally made of doctors, but have branched out to include other professions. Some are police officers, journalists, lawyers, and many more. In the renowned soap opera, the Horton family has remained to be the most revered in Salem.

Interestingly, Horton Square in Salem is named after the family and has a plaque dedicated to the founders, Tom and Alice Horton. The family is an upper middle class, Anglo-Saxon Christian household that resides in Salem and has a rich history.

Since Days of Our Lives premiered in 1965, the Horton family has been at the centre of the show's exciting narrative. They have a great lineage of six generations, family members whose stories are intertwined with other Days of Our Lives characters.
