Zendaya poses in 'barely there' diamond bra and Tom Holland's 'fanboying'

Publish date: 2024-09-02

Zendaya celebrated her 27th Birthday over the weekend and gave Instagram a present, as she posed in a tiny diamond bra. Of course, her long-term boyfriend Tom Holland was the first to show her support.

Although the Hollywood couple tends to keep their relationship out of the spotlight, the rare times they do show their love to each other via social media, fans go wild.

Zendaya stuns in birthday look with tiny diamond bra

Taking to her Instagram, Zendaya showed off her jaw-dropping birthday look, as she stunned in a tiny diamond triangle bra.

On top, she wore a long-sleeved cut-out crop top which revealed her torso, and a matching flowy skirt with a pink and purple swirling pattern.

Her bob was slicked back into a neat pony, and the star didn’t need any accessories for this look, as the statement outfit didn’t need any help.

Fans call Tom Hollands reaction ‘certified boyfriend material’

As well as admiring the actress in the comments, fans couldn’t help but comment on how cute Tom was for always being the first to comment and like his girlfriend’s pictures. It comes just a week after Tomdaya made a rare public outing at the newly turned 27-year olds hometown.

Of course, Tom was the first out of the thousands of comments, as he simply penned: “First.”

“I love your love for each other,” replied one fan.

Another joked: “Tom’s a bigger fanboy than any of us.”

“Tom instantly liking the post is so supportive boyfriend of him,” penned another.

“If you see a picture of Zendaya and there’s only one like, you’ll be sure that that one like will be Tom Holland’s like,” commented another.

Another joked: “I really thought I was going to comment before Tom.”

Zendaya celebrates 27th Birthday with adorable throwback

To celebrate turning the big 2-7, the Euphoria actress took to her 185 million Instagram followers to post an adorable throwback of herself as a child, as she shows off her missing teeth with a cheeky smile.

In the caption, she wrote: “Every year I grow I’m reminded of how precious this life is, thank you all for helping me fill it up with so much love. Your kind words and constant support mean everything to me. Thank you so so much and here’s to 27.”

Fans and celebrity friends took to the comments to wish the star happy birthday, as ex-Disney co-star Bella Thorne penned: “Happy birthday Z! [red love heart emoji]”.

Holland himself posted a birthday tribute to his girlfriend on his Instagram stories, sharing a photo of her in a wetsuit and snorkels, as he penned: “My birthday girl.”
