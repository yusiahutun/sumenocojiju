Alan Bersten Bio, Wiki (Early Life)

Minnesota native, Alan Berten was born on 26th May 1994, in Minnesota to Russian parents, as the youngest of three. As per nationality, he is an American, having a Russian-American ethnic background. Bersten started dancing at the young age of 7 and was taught by his elder brother.

Talking about his academic background, Bersten graduated from Hopkins High School and then attended the University of Minnesota. Growing up, Bersten became expert in Broadway, Bollywood, hip-hop, contemporary, jazz, hip-hop, Bollywood and Broadway. His diverse dances led him to win Youth Championship and Under 21 National Titles.

Alan Bersten Had a Surgery

In 2018, while Bersten was on tour with Dancing with the Stars: Live: he started to have cruel stomach pain which would last for about a minute and then vanish. After that, he went to check up at the hospital, where he was told that his blood had a high level of calcium and it’s probably not a big deal.

However, his mother pushed him to go back to the doctor for another blood test. Later a week, the report showed he was discovered with the symptoms of a tumor on one of his parathyroid gland called hyperparathyroidism. The following day, Bersten had surgery to remove his left parathyroid. After a week of resting, Bersten was back at work.
