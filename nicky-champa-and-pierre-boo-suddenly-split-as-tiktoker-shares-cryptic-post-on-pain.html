Nicky Champa and Pierre Boo suddenly split as TikToker shares cryptic post on 'pain'

Publish date: 2024-07-20

TikTok couple Nicky Champa and Pierre Boo have announced their split as Nicky shared a cryptic Instagram post about “pain”.

The famous online influencers and “relationship goals” couple have confirmed their breakup. They announced the news on Instagram, leaving many of their fans and social media followers heartbroken. Nicky shared a cryptic post on his profile and opened up about their split in an interview.

Nicky Champa and Pierre Boo split after six years

The TikTok stars have called it quits after a romance for six years. Nicky and Pierre were married and tied the knot in a low-key ceremony in Las Vegas in October 2022.

Pierre announced their breakup in a short Instagram Story last weekend and wrote: “We broke up. I am so sorry everyone.”

The breakup news left their fans devastated, as many expressed their disbelief about the end of their romance. Nicky and Pierre run a popular YouTube channel together and have 13 million fans each on TikTok.

“I no longer believe in love,” one heartbroken fan said as another wrote: “They were so cute together.”

“I really hope Nicky and Pierre sort everything out and are able to move on without any problems,” someone else said. “Whether it’s together or apart, I wish them happiness.”

Nicky hints at “pain” in an Instagram post

After officially announcing the breakup on Instagram, Nicky shared a cryptic post and hinted at “pain through my fame” with music lyrics from a song.

The TikToker shared a screenshot of the lyrics of The Irrepressibles’ song In This Shirt without any caption or message. Part of the lyrics says “I am lost in a rainbow and “now our rainbow is gone” as “our worlds move on”.

The bittersweet song also includes the following lyrics:

There’s a pain, it does ripple
Through my frame, makes me lame
There’s a thorn in my side
It’s the shame, it’s the pride
Of you and me, ever changing

Nicky speaks out on their breakup

Apart from the cryptic social media post, Nicky officially announced his split with Pierre in a media interview and insisted that Pierre would always hold a special place in his life.

He opened up about their breakup in an interview with Out Magazine, telling the outlet: “With social media, it’s a bit complicated. Our relationship became a brand.”

The TikToker continued: “I want to say I have nothing but love and respect for this person. I spent my entire twenties with this person. I grew up with this person. He’s a very special person and will always remain that way for me.”
