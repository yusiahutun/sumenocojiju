Michael van Gerwen produces 'worst performance of career' - including dart into single 12

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Michael van Gerwen shocked darts fans with what was labelled the "worst performance of his career".

The seven-time Premier League winner and current league leader exited at the quarter-final stage for the second straight week in Brighton on Thursday (March 7) night, losing 6-2 to Rob Cross.

It was all too easy for 'Voltage', who averaged below a ton but still outplayed Van Gerwen - whose poor run of form reach a new low in the eyes of many watching on at home, especially as he hit single 12 when aiming for T20.
