Towie star Demi Sims looks completely different before cosmetic surgery

Publish date: 2024-07-06

TOWIE'S Demi Sims showed off her natural beauty in a throwback picture taken on the day of her high school prom.

The reality star, 25, who recently removed her lip filler, can be seen flashing a beaming smile as she posed with brother Charlie Sims.

Demi praised her "decent" outfit which saw her sport a cream ballgown studded with sequins.

It was blended with a dark blue fabric that flowed up from the hemline.

Yet all eyes were on Demi's appearance, with her natural brunette locks piled high on her head a contrast to her recent platinum blonde bob style.

The Towie star's face was also filler-free and she opted for minimal make-up, with black eyeliner and pink lipgloss.

It showed Demi with her mouth wide open in delight as she scooped the gong for "biggest drip" of the school year.

She added the words: "So embarrassing when I had to collect the award in front of everyone."

The telly beauty recently showed off her completely different look after having her filler dissolved.

In an exclusive interview with The Sun, the reality star said she was thrilled with the results of her new lips and admitted getting her fillers dissolved was the "best thing she's ever done."

She proudly showed off her stunning new look after she told us that "natural is the way forward."

Speaking exclusively to The Sun, Demi previously said: "I’m so happy I’ve had my lips dissolved.

"I was waiting over a year for them to dissolve naturally and they weren’t dissolving so I had to get them physically dissolved and it’s the best thing I’ve done, no more duck lips."

The TV babe admitted that she's left cringing when she looks at old photos before the fillers were removed and said that she regrets getting them done in the first place.

"I look back at my pictures now and I just think what was I doing," Demi continued.

"I just think more natural looking is the way forward."
