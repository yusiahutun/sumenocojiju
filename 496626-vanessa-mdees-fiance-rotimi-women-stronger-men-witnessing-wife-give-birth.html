Vanessa Mdee's Fianc Rotimi Says Women Are Stronger than Men After Witnessing Wife Give Birth

Publish date: 2024-09-03
  • Vanessa Mdee has welcomed her second child, a baby girl and her fiance Rotimi heaped praises on the Tanzanian singer
  • Rotimi appreciated how strong his wife was during the delivery and said women were stronger than men
  • Social media users congratulated them for welcoming a newborn while others thanked Rotimi for appreciating women's experience in child delivery

Tanzanian singer Vanessa Mdee has been blessed with another child and her man couldn't stop gushing over her birthing experience.

Vanessa Mdee, Rotimi welcome second child

Mdee's fiance Rotimi, a Nigerian-American singer took to Instagram to compliment his lover and women in general for their strength in bringing life to the world.

"I admire you all. This is just incredible just to watch how strong you are and how you handling this. Contractions. Man, as men we can't handle that. We can barely handle certain massages. This is crazy. To all the mothers out there, wow (while saluting), wow. You guys need all the praise," he said.

Rotimi gifts Mdee 6-bedroom mansion

The Atlanta-based Tanzanian songbird Mdee was in 2022 gifted a six-bedroom mansion by her fiancé Rotimi.

The house, which is located in Florida near Disney World, has five bathrooms, an exclusive yard, and is valued at KSh 56 million.

Vanessa disclosed that the couple hardly pays keen attention to Valentine's Day but the gift was one that will forever remain etched in her mind.

The couple welcomed their first child, Seven Adesuwa in September 2021, two years after the two started dating.
