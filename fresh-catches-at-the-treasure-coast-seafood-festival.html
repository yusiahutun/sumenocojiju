Fresh Catches at the Treasure Coast Seafood Festival

Publish date: 2024-09-29

Sink your teeth into local seafood at the Treasure Coast Seafood Festival at Causeway Cove Marina on Hutchinson Island. Sample all kinds of tastiness from the sea (think: conch chowder and Filipino shrimp pancit) during the November 6-8 festival. Grab a beer or a fun cocktail from the full-service bar and head over to one of two stages to enjoy live music from nine local bands. $7/adults, free/children ages 12 and younger; 601 Seaway Drive, Fort Pierce; 954.205.7813
