Demi Lovato performed I Will Always Love Youat Paris Hilton's wedding

Publish date: 2024-05-08

Demi Lovato performed a rendition of the classic love song I Will Always Love You at Paris Hilton and Carter Reum's wedding.

Photos capture the singer, 29, clad in all black, wowing guests at the reception with the powerful track.

On her website, Paris gushed that the performance brought 'so much emotion and love' to the festivities.

'Carter and I aren't much of dancers, but we've been practicing for weeks and I think we pulled it off! I loved Demi Lovato's performance of "I Will Always Love You." They were incredible. It brought so much emotion and love to the event.'
