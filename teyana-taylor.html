Table of Biography

Teyana Taylor is a talented American director, singer, songwriter, model, dancer, actress, and choreographer. Teyana Taylor is also famous as the celebrity wife of basketball player Iman Shumpert.

Early Life

Teyana Taylor was born on 10 December 1990 and she is currently 33 years old. She hails from Harlem, New York City, and her zodiac sign is Sagittarius.

Likewise, her full name is Teyana Me Shay Jacqueli Shumpert (née Taylor). Talking about her family, her parents are Nikki Taylor and Tito Smith.

Further, Taylor is of African-American and African-Trinidadian descent. From her father’s side, this personality has three siblings.

Taylor grew up with her mother, and at present, her mother is her manager too.

Teyana Taylor – Net Worth 2024

Moving on, singer-songwriter Taylor has a net worth of around $5 million US dollars.

Relationship Status

Teyana Taylor is a happily married woman. Her husband’s name is Iman Shumpert and he is a famous American free-agent professional basketballer.

In the year 2015, after Taylor went into early labor, Shumpert assisted in delivering their baby girl.

Then on 20 September 2016, the singer hinted on The Wendy Williams Show that the couple may have married secretly. However, the lovely couple’s wedding was on 1 October 2016.

Then on 12 June 2020, the singer confirmed her 2nd pregnancy in the music video for her 2020 single Wake Up Love. 

On 4 December 2020, Taylor retired from music and shared this news through her Instagram page.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Teyana Taylor is a popular singer, actress, dancer, songwriter, model, choreographer, and director. In the year 2005, this personality signed a record deal with Star Trak Entertainment imprint.

It is a company of American musician Pharrell Williams. This was before Taylor’s 1st national appearance on MTV’s My Super Sweet 16.

Then in the year 2012, Taylor signed to Kanye West’s GOOD Music label through Def Jam. This was after the singer asked for her release from Star Trak.

As a songwriter, this personality has also written records for artists including Usher, Omarion, and Chris Brown.

Furthermore, the singer-songwriter appeared on runways during Fashion Week. Also, Taylor landed high-profile features including on Kanye West’s My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy.

Taylor also starred in the VH1 reality TV show Teyana and Iman. She appeared on this show alongside her husband Iman Shumpert – a basketball player.

More Details

In the year 2013, the singer signed a deal with Adidas. Following this, Taylor designed and released two pairs of sneakers.

The 1st pair released were the Harlem GLC and it was available in stores on 16 February 2013.

As shared by Jonathan Wexler – the Adidas Global Director of Entertainment and Influence Marketing – this personality holds the record for the fastest-selling sneakers in Adidas Originals history.

Following this, the singer focused on designing and releasing her 2nd pair of sneakers with Adidas. In March 2017, the singer-songwriter launched her “Fade 2 Fit” Workout Program.

It is a 90-day workout that centers around dance and fitness routines. Moreover, the singer also released a line of workout apparel called Fade2Fit.

Then in May 2019, the singer featured and directed the music video for Lil Durk’s remix of Home Body. It is a song from Lil Durk’s new album Signed to the Streets 3.

At the Met Gala 2021, Taylor wore the dress “Sleek in Silver⁠” – an Atelier Prabal Gurung Metallic Silver Draped High-Slit Skirt.

The dress had a Dramatic Train with Draped bra cups connected with Hand Embroidered Swarovski Crystal Straps⁠.

The designer behind the beautiful dress was the Nepalese-American fashion designer – Prabal Gurung. Then for the same event in 2022, she wore the dress “Gilded Galaxy” by the designer Iris van Herpen.
