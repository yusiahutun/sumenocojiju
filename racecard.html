Race Cards | HK Racing

Publish date: 2024-07-24

SIXTH GENERATION (G120) jumped awkwardly. When questioned regarding his riding in the early part of the Home Straight and as to whether there was an opportunity to improve between two runners sooner than he did, rider stated that after following the 4th placegetter and having a runner positioned to his outside, he commenced to shift out with a view to obtaining clear running to the outside of the 4th placegetter. He said when the forementioned runner did not continue to give ground as he had expected, he was then held up for clear running between the 400M and the 300M before shifting to the inside of the 4th placegetter to secure clear running. He added that when the 3rd and 4th placegetters shifted ground slightly, his mount raced tight between those two runners between the 300M and the 200M. Rider further stated that his decision to shift out initially was influenced by the fact that he felt the 3rd placegetter would shift out, resulting in the run which was already tight closing and he was of the opinion his best option at that stage was to endeavour to improve to the outside - Race 599.

TRADITION (J111) jumped only fairly. Held up for clear running on the Home Turn - Race 616.

COMET SPLENDIDO (G071) shifted out at the start and was bumped - Race 596.

BEER PALACE (H361) noted to have blood at its left nostril due to a small abrasion at the Start. Examined by the vet and passed suitable to race. After the race was examined by the vet who said an endoscopic examination confirmed that the blood was present due to a small abrasion at its left nostril. Jumped only fairly and was crowded shortly after the start - Race 604.

SURE JOYFUL (G125) from a wide barrier was shifted across behind runners in the early stages. Near the 300M was bumped and became unbalanced - Race 573.

CAN'T GO WONG (H087) bumped on the hindquarters shortly after the start and then raced tight for a short distance thereafter - Race 613.

SUPER WISE DRAGON (G475) jumped awkwardly and then was bumped by a runner. From a wide barrier was shifted across behind runners in the early stages. After the race, rider reported that his mount was not suited by the track conditions which, whilst still rated as "Good", had effects of some rain which had fallen during the meeting. After the race was found to be a "roarer" which had previously been reported - Race 567.

ATOMIC BEAUTY (H066) near the 1500M was momentarily crowded. Lay out between the 250M and the 100M, resulting in its rider changing his whip through to his left hand - Race 573.

INVINCIBLE MISSILE (C037) was slow to begin - Race 617.

SUNNY BABY (E166) jumped awkwardly, became unbalanced when bumped - Race 617.

SNOWALOT (E397) after the race, rider stated that, in his opinion, his mount would be better suited on a firmer surface - Race 613.
