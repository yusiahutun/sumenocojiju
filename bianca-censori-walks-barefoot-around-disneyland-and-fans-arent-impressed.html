Bianca Censori walks 'barefoot' around Disneyland and fans aren't impressed

Publish date: 2024-04-05

After their many dates to the Cheesecake Factory, Kanye West and Bianca Censori decided to switch it up and headed on a date to Disneyland and the former architect’s look may surprise you. Especially, her footwear, or, lack of.

The celebrity couple definitely aren’t shy. Bianca Censori is frequently photographed in some questionable outfits, showing more than your average person. Kanye West seems to encourage the risqué looks, although he likes to cover up himself. Although it doesn’t seem like the two can ever read the room, for once, they may have gotten the memo as they parade around the children’s theme park.

Kanye West and Bianca Censori take ‘date night’ to Disneyland

The couple were spotted hand in hand walking around the Los Angeles park this week, although they didn’t seem to be joined by Kim Kardashian and Ye’s kids.

They could be seen smiling and laughing as they wandered around the theme park, bombarded by fans wanting to get a shot of the couple on their mobile phones.

The music star tried to keep it low-key with a pair of shades, and a tracksuit, but this time opting for a cream color rather than his usual black.

The 29-year-old also kept it quite tame, compared to her usual looks. She wore a nude skin-tight dress, which some would compare to Kim Kardashian’s SKIMS.

On top, Bianca wore a brown leather panel. Previously, she’d worn similar versions but without anything underneath, so like we said, this look was tame for her!

Although her outfit was ‘pretty standards’ in her terms, her ‘shoes’, were not. Ye’s wife walked around in ‘socks’ with her toes peeking out. Let’s hope that the concrete wasn’t too hot!

“She’s at Disneyland barefoot???????” questioned one shocked X, formerly known as Twitter, user.

“No way Bianca at Disneyland barefoot. No f***** way,” wrote another.

Known for their ‘unique’ fashion sense, of course, this isn’t the first time the couple have been spotted barefoot.

One of the most memorable moments was last Summer when the two paraded around Italy with no shoes on.

The ‘barefoot’ trip came just a day before Adidas’ sale of leftover YEEZY sneakers after he was dropped from the brand for his anti-semitic remarks.
