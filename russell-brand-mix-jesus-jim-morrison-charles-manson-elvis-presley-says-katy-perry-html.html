There aren't many women who would admit their boyfriend reminds them of a serial killer.

But that is exactly how Katy Perry has described her fiancé Russell Brand.

The singer says the comedian's look is like jailed cult leader Charles Manson, mixed with Doors musician Jim Morrison, Jesus and Elvis Presley.

The boss: Katy Perry says fiancé Russell Brand reminds her of Jesus, Charles Manson, Elvis Presley and Jim Morrison

The 25-year-old also said Russell was 'the boss' in their relationship, but admitted: 'I like that. I've been waiting for someone I couldn't steamroll.'

Russell proposed to Katie on New Year's Eve in India, giving her a ring with two rubies and engraved 'the one I've been waiting for.'

Katy told Nylon magazine: 'I love it. I'm glad he didn't go for the "every other b**** in Hollywood is wearing it as well" ring.'

Celebrity lookalikes: Katy says Russell Brand looks like a mix of Doors singer Jim Morrison and cult leader Charles Manson

She said the proposal, which came three months after they met, 'involved fireworks, elephants, Indian dancers, horse-drawn carriages, and tens of thousands of flower petals.'

Katy was talking to Nylon magazine

But despite being devoted to Russell, Katy says marriage will not change her.

'I'm marrying one of the world's most scandalous men. How is that toning down?' she said.

Things seem to be going well for Katy and Russell, who are buying a house in Los Angeles together.

She has written a song about him  called Not Like The Movies for her next album.

Katy said her relationship with the 34-year-old had affected her songwriting.

'Sometimes I really feel like I'm more of a Taylor Swift... Love has really affected my songwriting.

'I know how to handle a man now.'

Katy also said her breakthrough song I Kissed A Girl took her by surprise.

'It was never meant to be a Girls Gone Wild song,' she said.

'It was meant to be fun, sweet, cute, kind of tongue in cheek... but now I'm comfortable with saying, "Actually, I've been with a woman."'
