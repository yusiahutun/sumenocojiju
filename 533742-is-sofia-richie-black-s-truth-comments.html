Who is Sofia Richie?

She is an American social media personality born on 14th August 1998 in Los Angeles, California, USA. Who is Sofia Richie's biological father? Her biological father is Lionel, while her mother is Lionel's second wife, Diane Alexander.

Her father is a legendary singer, songwriter, record producer, and TV personality. Does Sofia Richie have any siblings? Yes. She has one elder sister, whom her father adopted as a toddler with his first wife, Brenda Harvey, and a biological brother, Miles Lionel.

Sofia attended Oak Christian School, popularly known as Celebrity High. However, she left the school before graduation for homeschooling while her father was on musical tours. She was a soccer player until she was 16 when she broke her hip in a Segway accident.

Is Sofia Richie black?

The media personality identifies as black despite her mixed-race heritage. Her father, Lionel Ritchie, has a mixed ancestry of African Americans. On the other hand, her mother Diane Alexander's heritage is English, Scottish-Irish/Northern Irish and German.
