They were seen boarding a luxurious private jet on Saturday en route to a mystery location.

And it seems the final destination was the place where Jennifer Lopez would help celebrate her boyfriend Beau Casper Smart's 27th birthday that evening.

In a photo uploaded to Instagram, the 44-year-old I Luh Ya Papi singer can be seen hugging her much younger love as he closes his eyes - or is that a cringe? - and makes a wish before the enormous cake, which features an image of his half-naked self.

In the sweet snap uploaded to the Instagram account of party guests Steven Gomillion and Dennis Leupold - a photography team - a glimpse was given into how the night must have panned out.

Casper can be seen wearing a black T-shirt in the darkened shot, smiling with his eyes shut in front of the enormous birthday cake.

J-Lo stands proudly behind her man, with her head resting on his shoulder and one elegantly manicured hand wrapped around him and planted on his pecs.

Way to celebrate! The dancer and choreographer was gifted with a cake that featured an image of his own ripped body front and centre

The cake organized for the professional dancer and choreographer was not for the faint of heart.

The sizeable rectangular slab of deliciousness featured a photo of the man himself, shirtless and in prime form with his flawless six-pack on show.

Surrounding the photo are the words 'Happy Birthday, Beau!' plus two lines of silver fiery sticks, which burned more like firecrackers than mere birthday candles.

Nice pins! Jennifer Lopez boarded a private plane in Los Angeles on Saturday wearing a thigh-high mini dress and black heels

Clearly, Jennifer is not one to cut corners when it comes to celebrating her loved ones.

The elaborate birthday party fell the evening after the couple had boarded a swanky jet plane - after arriving at the airport in a Rolls Royce - to head out of Los Angeles.

The songstress got together with Casper shortly after calling time on her marriage to singer Marc Anthony, 45, the father of her six-year-old twins Max and Emme, in July 2011.

Travel companion: JLo's beau Casper Smart accompanied the diva on her trip

But after profile romances with the likes of actor Ben Affleck and rapper P.Diddy, Jennifer insisted she is in no rush to head down the aisle again following her doomed unions with ex-husbands Marc Anthony, Cuban waiter Ojani Noa and backing dancer Cris Judd.

In an interview with the April issue of InStyle magazine, she said: 'In the past, love for me has always meant forever and sure you still nurse those fantasies, but I don't try to force it anymore.

'I hung on to my fairy tale ideals for a long time. But where I am now, what I've been through, there are no rules. There are lots of ways it can turn out instead of just one. There are so many different kinds of happiness, not just the one you learned about when you were five years old.'

Style: The beauty and her boytoy arrived in a black and silver Rolls Royce

In the past, Jennifer has spoken about Casper, the prospect of the two of them walking down the aisle together, and what it would take.

'For me, it’s about having a great partner to walk this life with. I have my own high standards for what I want in a partner and how I want to be treated,' she explained in an interview with Cosmopolitan magazine in September of 2013.

'I bring a lot to the table. I'm not talking about material things but what I have to offer as a person – love and loyalty and all the things that make a good relationship.'

Keeping it casual: Casper opted for more laid back outfit, donning a black long sleeve shirt, dark wash jeans and a hat

She went on to reminisce over the initial period where she and Casper got to know each other.

'It's funny, early on with Beau (Casper), I remember telling him how I have a real button on being ignored,' she said. 

'And he was like, 'Listen, if somebody ignores you, then forget them. They don’t deserve to have your attention.'
