I've Been Living in These Ultra-Flattering, On-Sale Joes Jeans

Publish date: 2024-03-24

I might fall into this weird category of people who actually like wearing jeans. Sure, the rigid denim might have a bad rap; it’s tight, constricting, and just not comfy, but I’m here to tell you otherwise. I thought the exact same way as you, but that was all before I finally tried out Joe’s Jeans and realized that I was doing denim wrong my entire life (well, up to 29, that is).

I was inspired to test out the under-the-radar brand thanks to Jennifer Garner. A few years ago, the actress shared her fondness for Joe’s Jeans, which I didn't know about until then, which honestly, makes me sad because I really spent the latter half of my 20’s in jeans I didn’t really like. Alas, it’s never too late to make a change, which for me, meant adding Joe’s into my drawer — and I’ve never looked back.

In fact, one particular pair has been saving grace in recent months, so much so that I’d go so far as to say I’ve been living in them. You know how you have that one pair of pants — or even a top — that you reach for when you just don’t know? That’s exactly what these Joe’s skinny jeans are to me; they’re the pair I can whole-heartedly fall back on because I know that A) they’re comfortable, so I can wear them for whatever the day throws my way and B) they look good whether paired with sneakers, heels, or boots. Add in the fact that they lift my butt to a whole new level of perky, and you can best bet I’ll be wearing these all the time. Sorry, not sorry.

These particular jeans are made from a cotton-spandex blend that ensures durability but with just the right amount of stretch. For me, denim must have a little bit of give, because there’s nothing worse than feeling confined to limited movements when wearing pants. I may have accepted this in the past, but after a certain age (ahem, 29), I simply said no to uncomfy jeans. And that’s why I said yes to Joe’s jeans. Not to mention, the stretch fabric has some sort of magic sewn in, because my butt looks so good whenever I wear them.

While I currently have been spending most of my days in this particular pair, I’m ready to expand my collection, and the timing couldn’t be more perfect, as so many styles are on sale on Amazon right now. It makes sense, given its Big Spring Sale is coming up, and Joe’s is usually one brand that gets some heavy discounts.

Shop more flattering jeans from Joe’s while they’re on sale on Amazon. You’re welcome.
