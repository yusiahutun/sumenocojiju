Ivanka Trump's wedding guest dress is a famous copy - did you spot it?

Publish date: 2024-07-02

This is incredible…

    Former United States President Donald Trump's daughter Tiffany married her longtime partner Michael Boulos at the weekend and we really loved looking at the stunning wedding photographs.

    READ: Donald Trump's daughter Tiffany Trump sparkles as she marries Michael Boulos

    Tiffany's half-sister Ivanka was one of several bridesmaids, and they all wore various blue grecian-style gowns that looked beautiful.

    WATCH: Melania and Donald Trump unveil Christmas decorations at the White House

    Ivanka's dress was actually identified online as a fashion copy of a very famous film star's number. It was Grace Kelly! The actress wore an incredibly similar style in the smash hit 1955 movie and we think you will agree; it's uncanny! It has the same ruched bodice and vibrant two-tone colourway, as well as an ethereal cape. It even had the same jewelled spaghetti straps. Wow!
