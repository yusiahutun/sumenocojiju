Richest Real Housewives: The top 10 richest stars in the franchise

Publish date: 2024-02-23

The Real Housewives is a media franchise consisting of 11 television series as of 2021, which follow the lives of some rich housewives living in different American states. One thing about the women in the series is that they lead luxurious lives and live in million-dollar homes. But who are the richest Real Housewives in the franchise?

Real Housewives began in 2006, with its pilot being The Real Housewives of Orange County. It became a success and started documenting the lives of housewives in New York City, Atlanta, New Jersey, D.C., Beverly Hills, Miami, Potomac, Dallas, and Salt Lake City.

10 richest Real Housewives

The women of the Real Housewives franchise love to splash money and live it up in multiple luxury homes. They are no strangers to excessive wealth, and many are worth over $1 million. Who is the richest of all the Real Housewives? Here is a look at the top ten:

1. Kyle Richards - $100 million

Who is the richest Real Housewife? Kyle Richards is the wealthiest cast member of The Real Housewives franchise. She has an estimated net worth of $100 million. She is also on the top list of the richest real housewives of Beverly Hills. She receives about half a million dollars in wages for every season she appears in the show.

She has appeared in 211 episodes of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. Kyle Richards is also an actress who began acting as a child in 1974. Apart from her television career, she also makes money through her boutiques and fashion lines.

2. Carlton Gebbia - $100 million

Carlton Gebbia is one of the wealthiest in the franchise, with an estimated net worth of $100 million. She joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills in season 4 but left the series in 2014. Many people know her for embracing some elements of traditional witchcraft in the show.

3. Lisa Vanderpump - $90 million
