I Have an Eating Disorder

Publish date: 2024-03-02

Amanda Bynes has been getting some serious media coverage lately due to her bizarre appearance and random tweets -- but now she just dropped a new bombshell.

On her Twitter page last night, the former Nickelodeon star revealed she's been struggling with her weight.

"I'm suing certain blogs and magazines saying I have a mental illness," she posted on Thursday evening. "They take pictures anytime I've gained weight then write a fake story!"

The truth, according to Amanda: "I have an eating disorder so I have a hard time staying thin."

She didn't give any additional details, except to say that she hates the paparazzi photos used in reports about her.

"Only use photos from my twitter when writing an article about me," she pleads in another tweet.
