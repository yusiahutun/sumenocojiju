The Bold Version of This Jennifer Lopez-Worn Bag Is Going to be Big

Publish date: 2024-03-11

If I could look into a crystal ball and predict the future, I’d say that metallics in fashion will be all the rage through the holidays, at the very least. The shiny finish popped up everywhere in fashion this past spring and summer, making its shimmering luster a mainstay on purses, shoes, and clothing. After previewing designers’ upcoming 2024 collections at New York Fashion Week (NYFW), the trend isn’t going anywhere anytime soon. 

Coach is diving head first into the metallic trend with its new Shine Collection, which has been spotted on the likes of Jennifer Lopez and Dove Cameron. Coach’s most popular handbag silhouette, the Tabby Shoulder Bag, has gotten a bold new look and is now available in a high-shine silver — and we’re certain it’s about to be fall’s new ‘it’ purse. 

Coach Tabby Shoulder Bag 20

Lopez wore the silver style while sitting front row at the Coach NYFW show at the New York Public Library and paired it with knee-high snakeskin boots, a beige oversized fringe suede jacket, silver jewelry, and cream sunglasses. And this isn’t the first time the singer has been spotted with the handbag. She was named a Coach ambassador back in 2019 and has since worn the bag for years and appeared in several campaigns, including the 2023 Mother’s Day campaign and the current Shine Campaign, amidst wearing the silhouette during more casual street strolls.   

We’ve seen the timeless Tabby silhouette get several facelifts since its initial launch in 2019, including the TikTok-viral pillow style, a Y2K sheer jelly finish, and denim. The new metallic version features a detachable handle and shoulder strap for wearing as a handbag, a shoulder, and a crossbody style. The interior features several compartments for organizing your necessities, including a zippered compartment for securing your wallet or keys and an exterior back pocket for additional storage. The brand recommends storing the bag in a cool, dry place since the metallic mirrored leather is prone to scratches, sensitive to heat and humidity, and can wrinkle over time if not cared for properly. 

One shopper said the bag is a “perfect size” and fits all of their must-haves. Another reviewer called the style “eye-catching” and added it’s “everything and more.” While a third person mentioned that they receive “many compliments” while wearing the style and described it as “shiny” and “unique.” 

To recreate Lopez’s fashion week style, grab the Coach Tabby Shoulder Bag in the new metallic silver hue, and keep scrolling for more Tabby silhouettes in various fabrics, textures, and patterns.
