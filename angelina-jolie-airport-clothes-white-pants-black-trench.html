Angelina Jolie Puts an Elevated Spin on Airport Clothes

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Angelina Jolie is putting an elevated spin on airport clothes.

The actor and director was seen walking through New York City’s JFK airport with children Pax, 19, and Zahara, 18, on Friday. As usual, Jolie looked casually glamorous. Her travel outfit was made up of a sleek black trench coat belted at the waist, big white pants, and beige platform sandals with white soles. She accessorized simply with black sunglasses, diamond stud earrings, and gold rings, and was seen rolling a silver carry-on from Rimowa.

Pax looked cool in a classic white T-shirt, black jeans, and salmon-colored sneakers. He carried a black backpack and was seen rolling a navy suitcase.

Zahara was casual in a gray sweatshirt layered over a slinky gray top, black jeans, and black Converse sneakers. She wore several necklaces and carried a black backpack.

TheImageDirect.com

Jolie regularly showcases her timeless, elegant style during outings with her children. Last month, she debuted two minimalist ensembles while in New York City with Pax.

On one occasion, she looked fantastic in a chic white trench coat, which she layered over a black midi dress and paired with sheer tights, black pointed-toe pumps, a white leather crossbody bag, and large black sunglasses. She further accessorized with small gold hoop earrings and gold rings.

Another time, Jolie wore a breezy black wrap dress with bulbous long sleeves and a built-in belt. She paired it with matching black leather pumps and a black leather quilted pouch by Yves Saint Laurent, and added some glam with oversize round brown-and-gold sunglasses. She wore minimal makeup, a glossy brown lip, and her hair was down in an elegant blowout.
