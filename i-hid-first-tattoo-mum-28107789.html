'I hid my first tattoo from my mum for 6 months now I'm covered in ink'

Publish date: 2024-03-17

A tattoo fanatic who fell in love with inkings admitted she was so scared revealing her first to her mum she hid it for six months.

Taylor Paige Courtenay has so many tattoos that she has lost count of exactly how many she's had done.

From inkings of animals, to tatts featuring quotes and patterns, her appearance has often sparked discussion in public particularly from the older generation who say she's "ruined herself".

"I started with my first tattoo which is a gypsy lady’s head back when I was actually 17, I actually hid this from my mum for over six months because I knew she would hate it," Taylor said.

"But I have always known from a young age, throughout school and college that I just adored the art, I did actually always grow up wanting to be a tattoo artist."

Having studied Fine Arts in college, Taylor loves the designs and intricate patterns in tattoos.

"But in all honesty," she admitted. "As much as I love it I'm very squeamish and the idea of doing it on someone’s skin and seeing blood freaks me out (weird for someone who loves it herself I know)."

Out of all the tattoos she's had done, a select few of them have are especially significant to Taylor.

She has a phrase "Own your own story" tattooed across her chest which she sees as a moral for "whatever happens to you is supposed to for a reason".

On the bottom of her leg, she has a drawing of a view outside a plane window because she loves travelling.

Her grandparents also play a huge part in her life so one inking features a small piece of writing from them in dedication.
