Boxing icon Butterbean got 'really excited' during sex and 'ripped his penis in half'

Publish date: 2024-04-11

Boxing legend Butterbean once revealed how he got really excited while "fooling around" and ripped his penis in half.

Butterbean - real name Eric Esch - made the astonishing revelation during a chat with the Hannibal TV YouTube channel last year. "I think I'm going to have surgery pretty soon," he began during the interview in November 2021.

"I had something happen just recently that has never happened before. Um, it's kind of a delicate situation. It's very painful, trust me," he continued before detailing exactly what had happened. "So, we [Libby, his wife] hadn't fooled around in a week or so, a week or a week and a half, and I got really excited."

"I got over excited and my penis split and like, ripped in half," he said, prompting a shocked reaction from show Rick Bassman, who even took himself off camera as he contemplated the situation.

"It ain't funny, this is bad," Butterbean reiterated, struggling not to smile. "The skin just ripped in half. I don't know what to do. I haven't gone to the doctor yet, I'm kind of embarrassed about it but I don't know what to do."

"I mean, it's like, quite painful, believe it or not. It ain't funny my friend," he concluded as the three-man panel moved onto another, less sensitive subject.

Butterbean has had more than a few scrapes when it comes to bedroom antics. In another chat with Hannibal TV he revealed how he was once a sex addict.

"When you're the fat kid in school and always got picked on and never went on a date, I mean it was more of a challenge than anything," he said.

"My addition was never really drugs, not that I didn't do them, I'll be honest but my addition was sex. I had a sex addition that was crazy. I mean my wife knows about it so I can talk about it freely, but it was bad."

"I've overcome that I mean kind of like, boss you just got to quit doing it," he added. Butterbean hasn't stepped foot in a boxing ring since 2013 but he hinted at one last dance on social media earlier this year.

"I’m working with DDP [Diamond Dallas Page] to get up walking and running around again and I’m feeling the best shape I ever have," he said.

"I’m at fight weight. You’ll have to listen to my podcast coming up to find exactly how much weight I have lost, but I haven’t weighed this in over 20 years so that’ll give you an idea.

"The big announcement is I’m gonna fight one last fight. It’s gonna be one year from now. Jake Paul, if you’re ready, I’m here for you my friend. Let’s get it on, me and you Jake Paul.

"I bet it would be an awesome fight. After you said what you said about me in the magazine, I’m ready to fight you. Are you ready? I don’t think so."

Unsurprisingly, YouTube motormouth Paul has yet to respond to Butterbean's challenge.
