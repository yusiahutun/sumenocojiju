Festival season is only just gearing up. The Stagecoach country music festival is back this weekend on Friday, April 26 through Sunday, April 28 at the Empire Polo Club in Indio, California. Eric Church, Miranda Lambert, and Morgan Wallen are headlining the three-day event, and attendees can also expect to see Jelly Roll, Elle King, Leon Bridges, The Beach Boys, Brandi Cyrus, Wiz Khalifa, Diplo and more take the stage.

Stagecoach tickets have been available for quite some time; general admission and weekend passes are officially sold out on the festival’s website. But if you’re still on the hunt for last minute tickets, there are options available to ensure you don’t miss seeing your favorite country music stars live.

Stagecoach started in 2007 with headliners George Strait and Kenny Chesney. Since then, the festival has been attended by tens of thousands of fans, with a record 75,000 attendees at the 2018 Stagecoach. Unlike Coachella, which includes genres like rock, pop, indie, hip hop, and electronic dance music, Stagecoach is focused on country music and related genres, like folk, bluegrass, roots rock, and alternative country. The festival is also the highest-grossing country music festival in the world.

So how can country music fans still get access to Stagecoach tickets in 2024? Keep scrolling for everything you need to know about snagging tickets on resale sites, headliners, performers, and more—including a discount code to save on your passes.

How much are Stagecoach tickets?

How much are Stagecoach 2024 tickets? As of right now, tickets to the three-day festival are all sold out, but you can join a waitlist to see if passes open up last minute. If you aren’t willing to take your chances, three-day passes and single-day tickets are still available on a variety of ticket reseller sites, like StubHub, TicketNetwork, TicketSmarter, Ticketmaster, and Vivid Seats, the latter of which offers $20 off of orders of $200 or more with the code SC2022—a 10 percent savings.

While resale passes may end up costing you more than the original ticket release on the Stagecoach website, this is your best option for scoring passes the week of the festival. Resale prices for Stagecoach range from $422 to $651 for a GA three day pass, with Stubhub currently offering the most affordable prices.

    Where is Stagecoach 2024?

    Where is Stagecoach 2024? Stagecoach is held at the Empire Polo Club in Indio, California. The venue—where the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival is also held—is located at 81-800 Avenue 51 in Indio, and is a half-hour drive from Palm Springs, California, and a two-and-a-half hour drive from Los Angeles and San Diego. The venue was founded in 1987 and has 12 polo grounds, making it one of the largest polo clubs in the United States.
