'Floribama Shore' Star Nilsa Prowant Says Kayla Jo's Marriage and Pregnancy 'Doesn't Add Up'

Publish date: 2024-05-18

"Floribama Shore" star Nilsa Prowant is still salty about her roommate Jeremiah Buoni's summer fling with Kayla Jo.

TooFab talked to the reality star who said it's a little fishy that she's celebrating so many life milestones when they only filmed a few months ago.

"It's so funny that she's married and six months pregnant because that timeline sure doesn't add up," Nilsa told TooFab.

Nilsa was originally interested in Jeremiah, but once she realized that he was still going to choose Kayla Jo over her, she decided to devise a plan to get the "witch" out of the house. Kayla Jo told some of the cast in previous episodes that she was a Wiccan who "believed in a higher spirit besides God" and Prowant was the first roommate to become obsessed with the idea that she might be a witch.

But Nilsa put her faith in silicone, instead.

"All of my friends, we all have fake boobs, we all have fake lips so we're all bombshells," Nilsa said. "And so I was like, 'Alright, I gotta get this witch out of the house' so I was like, 'Who can I call to do the job? Which one of my friends do I need to call to get their attention off this girl Kayla Jo and get it onto my hot, cool, nice down to earth friends?'"

"I called the two people that I know best, Shawna and Katrina, and I'm like if anyone is going to get this girl out of the house, it's these two," Nilsa said.
