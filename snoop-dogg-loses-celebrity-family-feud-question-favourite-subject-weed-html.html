Snoop Dogg loses Celebrity Family Feud question on his favourite subject...weed

Publish date: 2024-08-26

He's well known for his expertise on the subject of marijuana.

But Snoop Dogg somehow managed to fluff a question about his favorite subject during a new episode of NBC's Celebrity Family Feud game on Sunday, according to Us Weekly.

The 44-year-old rapper-entrepreneur was up against former boxing champion Sugar Ray Leonard when host Steve Harvey asked the two stars to 'name something Grandma might do if she caught Grandpa smoking marijuana.'

Ready set....: Snoop Dog and his clan took on boxer Sugar Ray Leonard and his nearest and dearest on Sunday's episode of NBC's Celebrity Family Feud, hosted by Steve Harvey 

Snoop was the first to slam his hand down on the buzzer, causing much hilarity as the 59-year-old host poked fun at Sugar Ray's slow response.

'All them fast hands you got,' he joked, adding, 'Marijuana, bam! Just like that,' as he hit Snoop's lectern.

Unfortunately, the Drop It Like It’s Hot rapper, whose real name is Cordozar Calvin Broadus Jr., failed on what should have been his area of expertise.

His answer — 'Put hands on him,' meaning to hit Grandpa — was the No. 7 answer on the board.

Sugar Ray, 60, came up with 'she would scream at him,' the No. 2 response, and won the round.

Snoop was joined on the game by his wife, Shante, 44, their 19-year-old son Cordell and 17-year-old daughter Cori plus Snoop's rapper-actress cousin Robin Allen, 48, aka The Lady Of Rage.

But Snoop's mess up didn't cost the Broadus family the game. The clan ultimately won the quiz and $25,000 for the charity of their choice.
