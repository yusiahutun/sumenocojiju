Power | Articles and Latest News

Publish date: 2024-06-28
Power is a STARZ original series and the first installment in the Power universe. Power follows James "Ghost" St. Patrick, a wealthy New York City club owner and clever drug dealer who hopes to leave the criminal world and focus on club ownership and the NYC nightlife scene. The show also follows James' relationships with friend and partner in crime Tommy Egan, James' love interest and criminal prosecutor Angela Valdes, and former friend Kanan Stark. The series was nominated for multiple BET Awards and won the AAFCA award for Best Drama in 2019. Genre: Drama, crime Premiere Date: June 7, 2014 Cast: Omari Hardwick, Lela Loren, Naturi Naughton, Joseph Sikora, Shane Johnson, 50 Cent, and, Rotimi Spinoffs: Power Book II: Ghost, Power Book III: Raising Kanan, Power Book IV: Force, and Power Book V: Influence Read more about Power on STARZ.
