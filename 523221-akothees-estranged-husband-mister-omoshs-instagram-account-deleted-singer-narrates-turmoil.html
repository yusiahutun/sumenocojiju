Akothee's Husband Mister Omosh's Instagram Account Deleted as Singer Narrates Turmoil

Publish date: 2024-08-17
  • Barely 24 hours after Akothee shared a post revealing she had been going through a lot, her husband's social media accounts were deleted
  • Many expected Denis Schweizer, aka Omosh, to comment on his wife's post, offering her support, but that did not happen
  • Akothee and Omosh have been together for almost five months when fans started noticing the Mzungu man's absence on the singer's social media accounts

Akothee's Mzungu husband has left Kenyans confused after his social media accounts vanished.

Did Omosh delete his social media accounts?

Denis Schweizer, who was given the name Omosh by his Kenyan wife, had 72.8k followers on Instagram before his account was deleted. The Mzungu man's Facebook account is unavailable. It is unclear if the accounts have been deactivated or deleted permanently.

The profile was a photo of him and Akothee at their wedding, which was held in April at the Windsor Hotel.

"Digital content creator, entrepreneur, husband to Esther Akoth Schweizer Omondi the 7th," read his Instagram bio before the account vanished.

Akothee and Omondi were madly in love, and after their glamorous wedding, they travelled to Greece for their honeymoon. While celebrating her newly found love, the 43-year-old singer-turned-entrepreneur shared a photo from their wedding and captioned it:

"I am Mrs Schweizer. Tell the newcomers to watch my royal fairytale wedding on my YouTube channel. I create the wave. Mrs Schweizer, it is @misteromosh. I love you king."

Akothee reveals all isn't well

Akothee shared a lengthy post revealing she had been undergoing therapy and healing in private. The vocal musician-turned-entrepreneur said she has gone through a lot in the past couple of months and, at times, went without food.

She said she broke down several times while going through the dark time.

"I have come out of a very dangerous zone, and I have been healing in private. I am in my second month of therapy. The trauma I went through after finding out the truth and some ugly stuff left me shaking. I went for days without food and no sleep, questioning and answering myself. It has been very heavy some days, a night will just break into a day with me looking outside the window gazing at nothing. I would be shaking for reasons I can't explain..." she wrote in part.

The mother of five said she had to put on a brave face and pretend everything was okay, yet deep down, all was not well. She thanked her ex-manager, Nelly Okas, her children and a few friends for supporting her during the difficult time.

"Nelly Oaks has been on speed dial. He would call me nonstop and would panic when my phone went off, my children, especially the girls made sure we FaceTime just to check if I was okay. Rue followed me to Europe in fear of losing me, babysitted me until I could stand on my feet," she added.
