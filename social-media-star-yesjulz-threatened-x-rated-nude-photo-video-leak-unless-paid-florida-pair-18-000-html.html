YesJulz threatened by Florida model and friend over the release of X-rated images

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Social media queen 'YesJulz' is at the centre of an alleged extortion attempt after a Snapchat model and her accomplice threatened to leak nude photos and video of the star unless she paid them $18,000.

Police have reportedly alleged Florida pair, Hencha Voigt and Wesley Victor, sent several of the X-rated photos as proof to the star, whose real name is Julieanna Goddard, and gave her 24 hours to pay up.

Goddard, 26, is said to have contacted police about the plot and organised a fake meet-up on July 21 with Voigt and Victor.

Miami police mugshots of Hench Voigt (left) and Wesley Victor (right) after their arrest this week

Police officers found Voigt and Victor sitting in a car on Miami Beach waiting for the meeting as well as the phone used to allegedly threaten Goddard, according to a report in the New York Daily News.

Naturally, the case has now been playing out on social media in recent days. 

Voigt appeared to deny the extortion charges against her in a tweet.
