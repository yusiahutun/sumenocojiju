The face of Patti Stanger has really changed over the years.

The Millionaire Matchmaker, 51, has shared a picture of herself as a fresh-faced college graduate with fans on Bravo's website.

Of course, the flashback shot was taken well before her plastic surgery makeover.

Patti has gone under the knife a few times and has had her eyelids done, Botox injections and a breast reduction. She also appears to have veneers.

The outspoken TV dating expert isn't shy admitting it either and even went into detail about fixing her droopy eyelids - a condition called ptosis - during a recent appearance on Watch What Happens: Live.

'I wanted a facelift. He [her doctor] wouldn’t give it to me,' she told host Andy Cohen.

'We’re all getting plastic surgery,' she added. 'Come on, this is the game here, and HDTV exaggerates all the features. Yeah, I’m proud of it, because we’re all doing it. Nobody’s talking about it.'

She also stated that she gets Botox too, but that it's because of the cluster headaches she's prone to.

Going strong: Patti's current beau, David Krausse, calls the matchmaker 'beautiful'; Patti recently tweeted this cheerful 'family' picture on her Twitter page, writing: 'Happy Birthday Sammy! @disneyland'

Patti had more advice for those seeking cosmetic surgery, maintaining that the surgeons 'lie' about the recovery time required so 'you better double it.'

The TV star, who also runs the site Pattiknows.com, also had a breast reduction four years ago.

Meanwhile, Patti is in the throes of a new romance with David Krausse, whom she met online. She introduced him to friends at her 51st birthday bash at Koi last June. 

Shameless: Patti is proud of all the plastic surgery, saying, 'we're all doing it. Nobody's talking about it'

'His name is David [Krausse] and I met him online and he's a keeper,' the matchmaker told Us Weekly at the time, describing her guy as an 'ex-baseball player turned mortgage broker.'

The Millionaire Matchmaker, who had just ended a previous relationship, wasn't worried about diving in to the online dating scene.

'I tried four sites, including my own site and Plenty of Fish, and Plenty of Fish won,' Patti said. 'And I did everything in my book. I did dating detox... I knew what I wanted, he what he wanted.'

When asked what he likes about Patti, David gushed, 'There's a million things - she's beautiful. And obviously she has a heart of gold.'

Patti ended her seven-year relationship with real estate executive Andy Friedman in 2010.
