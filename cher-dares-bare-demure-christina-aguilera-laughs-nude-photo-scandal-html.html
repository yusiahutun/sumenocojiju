She might be more than twice her age but that didn't stop Cher flashing a bit more flesh than her twenty-something co-star.

The singer, 64, attended the Spanish premiere of her new film Burlesque in a very brazen outfit of a sheer top, see-through bra and short skirt.

In comparison her 29-year-old cast mate Christina Aguilera was more modestly dressed in a beaded black calf-length dress.

Burlesque babes: Christina Aguilera looked modest in her black calf-length dress compared to Cher's revealing sheer top and see-through bra

Although Christina was probably feeling more self-conscious than Cher in Madrid last night, a day after racy pictures of  the star hit the internet.

In the pictures, which appeared on several websites, Aguilera is seen posing inside what appears to be her walk-in closet wearing just body jewellery and nipple coverings.

The photographs were apparently hacked from an email account belonging to Christina's stylist.

Revealing: Cher's sheer top showed off a see-through bra that left very little to the imagination

'The photos of Christina Aguilera being leaked to the press were illegally obtained by a hacker who tapped into Christina's personal stylist's account,' a representative for the singer told The New York Daily News.

'The photos were taken in the privacy of Ms Aguilera's home and were used only in a personal exchange between the star and her stylist.'

'We are attempting to determine the identity of the hackers and will pursue them aggressively,' the spokesman added. 'Their conduct is reprehensible.'

Covered up: Christina put on a brave face after racy pictures of  the star hit the internet

Cher has never been shy about showing off her surgically-enhanced figure and wore a very plunging short dress to the Burlesque premiere in Hollywood last month that revealed her bra underneath.

And in September this year, the actress wore a similar outfit to the very daring one she wore in the video for her 1989 song If I Could Turn Back Time.

Christina's happy mood could well have been due to her budding romance with Matthew Rutler, her assistant on the musical film.

Roles reversed: Cher kept it all covered up while Christina showed off her curves in a sexy red dress at the movie's photocall earlier in the day

Christina is set to return to Los Angeles later this week in preparation for the wedding of her friend Nicole Richie.
