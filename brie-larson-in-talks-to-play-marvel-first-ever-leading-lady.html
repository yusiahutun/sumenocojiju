Brie Larson in talks to play Marvel's first ever leading lady

Publish date: 0001-01-01

    Brie Larson is reportedly in talks to play the leading lady in which will be the first solo female lead film role to come out of the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

    According to, Brie is at the top of the list to play the superhero, with sources confirming that Brie is the "first choice," and that the actress is "leaning towards playing the part."
