Justin Moore and Family Welcome New Baby Girl!

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Justin Moore and wife Kate welcomed a new baby into their family on Monday (July 14), and for the third time, they heard "It's a girl!" when the bundle of joy was born.

"We’re very excited to welcome Rebecca Klein Moore into the world. Both Mom and Klein are healthy," the Moores say in a statement to People.

The baby, who is named after her paternal great-grandmother, will be called by her middle name. Klein weighed in at 6 lbs., 13 oz. at birth and was 19 and a quarter inches long. The sweet baby joins big sisters Kennedy Faye, 2.5, and Ella Kole, 4.

Moore has taken time out of his hectic touring schedule to help his family ease into life as a unit of five, and previously admitted that he and his wife were armed and prepared for the newborn to make his or her appearance. After all, they'd already done the newborn thing twice before.

The Moores did not find out the baby's sex -- the country singer shared that his wife didn't want to find out, and "she wins most battles." Of course, after two girls, many were wondering if the new baby would be a first-time boy. Moore himself has discussed his hopes for a boy someday, explaining that they've talked about as many as four or five kids. But don't think for a second that he was disappointed with another daughter; he also said that if a boy wasn't in the cards, he was fine with it.

No doubt, the 'Point at You' singer is thrilled to add another sweet girl to his brood -- he better start saving his pennies now for all the weddings he'll need to pay for!

Congratulations to the Moore family on their tiny addition!
