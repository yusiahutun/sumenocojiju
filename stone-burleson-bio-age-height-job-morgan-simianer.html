Who Is Morgan Simianer Boyfriend, Stone Burleson? Bio, Age, Height, Job

After Cheer became an overnight sensation on Netflix in early 2020, it is about time we get just as gripped by its second season that premiered on 12 January 2022. Now, you remember Morgan Simianer right? Yes. The cheerleader, who became a fan favorite due to her sweet personality and her troubled past. So, before you see Cheer Season 2 continue to follow the ups and downs of life at Navarro, let us spill to you that Morgan here has found a boyfriend for herself. He is Stone Burleson and this BIO here is everything you need to know about him.

Meet Morgan Simianer Boyfriend, Stone Burleson

Amid the initial Cheer (Season 1) press tour, Morgan Simianer was in a relationship with minor league baseball player Brad Markey. The pair had reportedly started dating in August 2018. They shared Instagram snaps from trips to Turkey, Greece, and New York City for two more years. But, eventually broke up without making an announcement.

Then, E! News reported that Morgan seemingly began dating Big Brother 21 winner Jackson Mitchie in December 2020. And this too lasted only until February 2021.

Enters Stone Burleson!

As it turns out, Morgan has been dating Stone since at least March 2021. “Guys I did it”, she captioned a photo with her new boyfriend in December 2021 and made it official with the “I found the person who makes me the happiest I’ve ever been.”

A couple of months into the relationship and they have already Instagrammed enough for a lifetime on each other’s pages.

Morgan would write something like “Nothing else I want for Christmas besides this one” as captions and Stone would also make just as impactful ones like “My beautiful, brown-eyed girl.” or on National Girlfriend Day, he would say “It’s National GF Day and how LUCKY am I to call this special, beautiful woman all mine. You’ve been a blessing in my life since I met you and I can’t wait to do forever with you by my side!”.

Stone Burleson Age

As Stone Burleson turned 26 years old in 2021, his mother took to her social media to lovingly write “My wish, for you, is that this life becomes all that you want it to/Your dreams stay big, your worries stay small/You never need to carry more than you can hold.” On his big day, she also confessed that she feels blessed, thanks to him.

Stone Burleson Height

Straight-out-of-magazine handsome Stone Burleson stands above 6′ 1” tall.

Stone Burleson Job

As per LinkedIn, Stone Burleson is currently (January 2022) working as a junior loan originator at NFM Lending in Arlington, Texas. He has been helping home buyers and homeowners find the best loans for their needs as of March last year.

Before that, from August 2019 – February 2021, Stone was a loan partner at Summit Funding, Inc.

And even before that, for a year, he was the account manager for BravoTECH.

Stone Burleson Family

Stone Burleson’s father Michael Burleson is an owner-operator at U.S. Best Nutrition and control room operator (CRO) at Paradigm Midstream. Also from what he states on his Facebook, he is from Glendive, Montana, and currently living in Arlington, Texas.

Then, there’s Stone’s mother Kimberly Spinks Burleson who says that she likes a little coffee with milk and sugar. On 21 June 2021, she took to her Instagram to write an anniversary message to her husband that read “From our 1st Anniversary to today’s 29th Anniversary, you still take my breath away every time you walk through our door. Thank you for choosing forever with me. I love you.”

For siblings, Stone has two sisters: Shane and Sunny Burleson.

All these folks have met Stone’s beau Morgan and loved her and from the look of it, Stone also has met his cheerleader girlfriend’s dad Bill, brother Wyatt, and the rest of the family.
