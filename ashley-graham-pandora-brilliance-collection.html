Ashley Graham looks dazzling wearing Pandora's new sustainably lab-created diamond jewellery

Publish date: 2024-05-24

In partnership with Pandora

    Supermodel and industry icon, Ashley Graham, alongside actress and producer Rosario Dawson, has teamed up with Pandora for the exciting launch of the brand’s Brilliance collection. Exclusive to the UK, this collection features sustainably lab-created diamonds in timeless designs, complete with dazzling stones ranging from 0.15 to one carat. Yes, you heard that correctly: diamonds!

    The model mogul stuns in the campaign photos, wearing elegant infinity-shaped earrings, rings, bracelets and necklaces from the collection. Boasting affordable prices for diamond jewellery, these pieces start from as little as £250, making them the perfect accessory investment.

    Speaking about the collection, Ashley revealed: “I love the empowering message of this collection, [which] symbolises infinite possibilities and growth. In my 20+ years in the fashion industry, I’ve seen such a positive shift towards inclusivity. While there’s still more work to be done, I feel hopeful that there is infinite possibilities for the future. I also love how Pandora is making diamond jewellery more accessible for everyday wear.”

    “The beauty of this collection is it can fit with any style and you can dress it up or down. My top tip is to stay true to yourself, but don’t be nervous to switch it up! Wear a few pieces at a time, or layer it up for a more impactful look.”

    Ashley stacks her Pandora Brilliance rings for maximum style points

    SHOP COLLECTION

    Following the certified 4C’s that ensure diamonds are of high quality – cut, colour, clarity and carat weight – Pandora’s Brilliance collection is, well, all round brilliant. The diamonds have the highest cut standard to maximise sparkle; they are near colourless; and they have VS2+ clarity meaning there are no visible flaws to the naked eye. Of course, precious stones require precious settings – so every piece in the collection is crafted from quality metals of solid 14k gold, 14k white gold or sterling silver.

    Ashley flashes a smile as she models the spectacular Pandora Brilliance collection

    But best of all is the sustainability factor. Hailed as the future of the jewellery industry, lab-created diamonds are cleverly grown in highly controlled laboratory environments. The end result is diamonds that hold the same quality they would have if they were mined, but just with a reduced impact on our planet’s ecosystem. Plus, Pandora’s collection in particular has been awarded a CarbonNeutral® certification to celebrate the transition to a low carbon global economy.
