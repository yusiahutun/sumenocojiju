Why Wasnt Travis Kelce at Taylor Swifts Eras Tour Film Premiere? Did They Split?

Publish date: 2024-03-31

Maybe he was in the getaway car. Many eagle-eyed Swifties noticed that Travis Kelce wasn’t at Taylor Swift’s Eras Tour film premiere.

The two have been spending a lot of time together. The 33-year-old pop star spent two weekends cheering on Kansas City Chiefs tight end Travis Kelce as his team faced the New York Jets and the Chicago Bears. Her first game—which took place at Arrowhead Stadium in Kansas City, Missouri, on September 24, 2023—came after weeks of dating rumors surrounding her and the NFL player. During the match, she was photographed cheering alongside Kelce’s mom, Donna, in a private suite after he scored a touchdown. Later that evening, Swift drove off in Kelce’s car and the pair reportedly attended an after party together with his teammates at a local restaurant.

Though all eyes are on the relationship, Kelce talked about why he thinks the NFL is overdoing their coverage in his podcast with his brother in the October 4 episode of New Heights. “Let’s get to it, is the NFL overdoing it?,” his brother Jason who plays for the Philadelphia Eagles, asked about the celebrity coverage. “What’s your honest opinion? Take away your feelings for Taylor…” which prompted a laugh from Travis.

Kelce said, “I think it’s fun when they show who was at the game. I think it brings a little bit more to the atmosphere, brings a little bit more to what you’re watching. But at the same time I think-“

“They’re overdoing it,” Jason finished, to which Kelce agreed: “They’re overdoing it a little bit for sure, especially my situation. [But] I think they’re just trying to have fun with it.”

So why didn’t Travis Kelce attend Taylor Swift’s Eras Tour film premiere? Read more to find out.
