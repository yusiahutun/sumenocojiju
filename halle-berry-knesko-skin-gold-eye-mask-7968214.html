Halle Berry Loves Knesko Skins $17 Gold Eye Masks

Publish date: 2024-05-04

I don’t know what type of night Halle Berry was recovering from when she posted a selfie to her Instagram Stories wearing gold eye masks with the caption “the morning after,” but I know the feeling — the feeling of waking up with a face that needs a little more TLC than your usual skincare routine. In Halle’s case, I didn’t even need to do any detective work to find the product; she also wrote in the caption, “Thank you @kneskoskin. I love these so much,” referring to Knesko Skin’s Nano Gold Repair Collagen Eye Mask. 

I was first introduced to the clean, reiki-centric beauty brand this past February when Pamela Anderson’s makeup artist said she preps her iconic pout with the brand’s Nano Gold Lip Mask. It isn’t a surprise that the two early 2000s icons are fans of the same range from the same brand once you spend some time reading through its ingredients and benefits. 

The Knesko Eye Masks are not simply colored gold — they also use 24-karat colloidal gold to promote antioxidants, heal skin and wounds, and improve blood circulation. Gold also has a slew of anti-aging benefits: It improves firmness and elasticity while preventing environmental and sun damage protection — both of which can speed up signs of aging. 

But wait — there’s more! The serum infused in the eye masks also contains hyaluronic acid, vitamin E, and collagen for an additional boost of intense moisture, hydration, plumping, and fine line- and wrinkle-fighting properties.

The fanbase for Knesko Skin’s Nano Gold Repair Collagen Eye Mask extends far beyond Berry. One shopper called these “life savers.” “My under-eyes are so bad, and the difference these make after a long day is invaluable,” they added. Another reviewer beamed, “[Knesko’s Gold Repair Eye Mask] helped my eye puffs so much and it helped smooth out all of the lines underneath my eyes that make concealer a challenge to put on… These can help you get the smoothest under-eyes.”

Head to Knesko to shop the Nano Gold Repair Collagen Eye Mask in one-pack or six-pack options.
