Chris Kattan's mug shot for his Monday arrest on suspicion of a DUI in LA has been released.

The 43-year-old looks almost unrecognizable from his heyday in the '90s when he was more chiseled as a regular on Saturday Night Live.

After video was released of the comedian stumbling through his field sobriety test, Kattan took to Twitter asking his followers to not ridicule him: 'If you don't have something nice to say, don't say it at all.'

Not guilty: Chris Kattan pleaded not guilty to DUI on Monday after crashing into a parked vehicle on the side of an LA freeway nearly three weeks ago

The Night AT The Roxbury star, who was questioned by police after crashing into a parked Department Of Transportation vehicle on an LA freeway, also told fans he was OK.

'I'm fine,' the funny man posted on Twitter. 'I'm lucky nobody was hurt.'

The star appeared wobbly as he was videotaped during a field sobriety test, as seen on TMZ. According to the site, the actor admitted to using prescription drugs.

Not so funny: The 43-year-old was arrested after hitting a parked car on the side of the freeway

Sad: The actor looked embarrassed as he struggled through a field sobriety test

On edge: At the Movie 43 premiere in 2013

'I just got back from a 15hr flight after touring out of the country. I was exhausted. The Police were so kind,' he explained in a Monday tweet.

'I passed all tests, released without bail, have drivers license, cop offered to drive me home,' he added.

The incident occurred at 1 am in the LA's San Fernando Valley on the 101 freeway, the same stretch Lamar Odom was arrested for a DUI in 2013.

Police were called after a woman noticed another car, a Mercedes-Benz, was weaving and driving at a slow speed, according to TMZ.

But it was Kattan who caught the police's eye after he plowed through freeway cones to careen into that parked vehicle that was left at the side of the road, the site reported.

No one was inside the DOT vehicle and after the accident Chris emerged unharmed but not coherent.

The star's blue sedan was smashed in the front, with its bumper torn off after the collision, as seen in the video footage.

Wearing a white T-shirt with the print of a bird on it, and grey Free City sweatpants, the personality appeared to be under dressed for the cool winter night.

On the video posted by TMZ, The How I Met Your Mother guest star looked like he was in shock as he held his hands in front of him and tried to not make eye contact with a highway patrol officer.

When asked to walk in a straight line, the celebrity stumbled after two steps, waving his hands in the air and nearly falling over.

The policeman had to help steady him.

Kattan was then arrested and booked into the Van Nuys jail.

Chris is best known not just for his 10 years on SNL, during which he worked with everyone from Gwyneth Paltrow to Steve Martin.

But he also had a movie career, which included the 1998 hit Night At The Roxbury with SNL collaborator Will Ferrell and 2013's Movie 43.

The LA native has three films ready for release: ImagiGARY with Jack Briggs and Charlie McDermott - he plays Officer Jones; Walk Of Fame with Scott Eastwood and Jamie Kennedy - he stars as Alejandro; and the short film Passenger with Justine Wachsberger.

He was a movie star at one point: In 1998's Night At The Roxbury with Will Ferrell (far left)

The actor has been working steadily since he got his start in the LA theater group The Groundlings in the early '90s. His father was also a member.

Kattan's mother Hajnalka Biro was a Playboy model.

The star has been in several relationships with other comedians. He dated fellow SNL star Maya Rudolph who he was spotted with on carpets often. In 1999 he was with Jennifer Gimenez, then in 2011 he dated Jennifer Coolidge.

In 2008 he married model Sunshine Deia Tutt, who he divorced the following year.
