Emmy Sharrett Bio, Age, Family, Dating

Publish date: 2024-03-20

Bravo TV’s new restaurant reality show, Southern Hospitality, follows Southern Charm resident boss-lady Leva Bonaparte and her larger-than-life staff at Republic Garden & Lounge. Leva and her husband, Lamar, own four restaurants along King Street, but Republic stands out as the crown jewel of their kingdom. Emmy Sharrett is one of the cast members of Southern Hospitality.

Learn about Emmy’s reality TV stint, age, net worth, dating life, and more in the wiki article below.

Emmy Sharrett On Southern Hospitality

Southern Hospitality premiered on Bravo TV on September 28th at 9 pm. Besides Emmy Sharrett, Southern Hospitality’s other cast includes Leva Bonaparte, Maddi Reese, Mikel Simmons, Grace Lilly, Joe Bradley, Lucia Pena, Mia Alario, Will Kulp, TJ Dinch, and Bradley Carter.

Ahead of the show’s premiere, Emmy shared a lot of BTS scenes from the shoot of Southern Hospitality and requested her followers to tune into the premiere by writing, “tune in 11/28 at 9 pm #SouthernHospitality @bravotv.”

As per Emmy’s bio on Bravo’s website, she recently graduated from the College of Charleston and is currently having a great time handling VIPs. Also, Emmy is no stranger to the intricacies of sisterhood at Republic.

Emmy loves her job but she is keeping an eye open for the next phase in her career. Although Emmy has not decided what to do next, she is pretty sure it will involve her boyfriend, Will.

Emmy Sharrett Age

Emmy Sharrett Family

Born to Brandon and Shawn Sharrett, Emmy grew up in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, as the older of four siblings, all sisters. Emmy’s parents married in October 1997 and gave birth to her in 1998.

Emmy’s mother, Shawn, is a partner and co-owner at CB Jewelry and a former worker at Elite Model Management. Her father, Brandon, on the other hand, is a General Manager and president of Corporate Solutions & YourCause at Blackbaud. Currently, he also serves as the vice president at Fiserv.

Brandon celebrated his birthday on February 7th. On his 49th birthday, Emmy wrote, “Happy 49th birthday to the most dedicated and supportive dad I could ever ask for. I love you with all my heart. Words can’t describe how appreciative I am for the sacrifices you’ve made for my happiness,” on her Instagram.

Coming to Emmy’s sisters, they are Madison, Montana, and Peyton. We don’t know much about them except that Peyton is currently pursuing undergrad studies and is expected to graduate in 2024.

Emmy’s sister, Montana, celebrated her birthday every 8th of May. And on May 8, 2020, Emmy wrote, “Happy birthday to the best older sister I could have ever asked for. Congrats on the perfect genetics and creating Lyla🙃💕 love you a long time,” for Montana.

Is Emmy Sharrett Dating Anyone?

Emmy Sharrett’s dating life is public and her social media followers know her boyfriend, Will Kulp, pretty well.

Will was born and raised in Charleston. However, he moved to New York to pursue modeling after a chance encounter with a scout. He eventually returned home and currently works at Republic Garden & Lounge. He stars on Southern Hospitality as a bartender alongside his girlfriend, Emmy Sharrett.

According to The Cinemaholic, Emmy and Will reportedly had a rough patch in Season 1 of the show when rumors of Emmy and the boyfriend of one of the workers at Republic began circulating. However, the rumor did not go to impact their relationship as the two look always content.

Will shares his birthday with Emmy and on their birthday, Emmy shared three pictures featuring her and her boyfriend on her Instagram and captioned it: ‘Happy birthday baby, I love you❤️ Wouldn’t want to share a birthday with anyone else.’

Will has featured Emmy two times o his Instagram. One time on August 14, 2022, he shared a clip of him pushing her girlfriend to what looked like a pool and captioned it, “Picture time is over @emmysharrett.”
