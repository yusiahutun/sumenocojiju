Publish date: 2024-01-31

So, who are her go-to designers?

6 of the most expensive jewellery sets spotted on Taylor Swift

1. Oscar de la Renta

The “Gorgeous” singer is clearly a fan of Oscar de la Renta, as it’s been her designer of choice on the red carpet on multiple occasions.

Take for instance her dazzling crystal sheer minidress from the MTV Video Music Awards last year. The head-turning ensemble decorated with silver chains was a bold move for the songstress, who rocked it boldly without skipping a beat. Swift complemented the US$25,000 piece with sparkling Louboutin heels and Lorraine Schwartz jewels.

A year earlier, she made history as the first female artist ever to win Grammy’s album of the year three times and she did it in an unforgettably charming floral dress. From Oscar de la Renta’s autumn/winter 2021 collection, this tulle dress is appliquéd with about 200 colourful blooms, and was the perfect choice for the floral princess.

For the premier of Cats in 2019, she picked another classy Renta number. She wore a satin gown covered in floral and leaf patterns with the orange-red and black theme from the brand. The gown’s palette was perfect for her character from the film – a ginger catwoman Bombalurina. It cost almost US$18,000, per Insider.

2. Elie Saab

The “Style” singer also favours bold colours and for that, she picks Elie Saab.

Swift channelled mermaid vibes back in 2015 for the Grammys in the brand’s shimmering emerald-turquoise gown. She matched it with a pair of opal Paraiba earrings and rings by her favourite jeweller, Lorraine Schwartz – worth an estimated US$1 million in total.

The “Red” singer appeared in another Elie Saab number when she was crowned as the “Woman of the Year” at Billboard Music Awards in 2012. She stunned in a red chiffon sleeveless gown with a lace bodice.

A year later, she once again chose a red-wine haute couture gown with sophisticated embroidery to receive the rare Pinnacle Award at the Country Music Awards for artists who take country music to a worldwide audience.

3. Atelier Versace

For even more vibrant colours, Swift often looks to Atelier Versace. In 2016, she arrived at the Grammys in a colourful two-piece ensemble: a coral top and a hot pink floor-length cutaway satin skirt.

Later in 2019, she opted for the Italian house again at two events during the same month of August. At the Teen Choice Awards, she combined her rainbow-printed bodysuit with a blazer from Versace’s pre-autumn 2019 Voyage Barocco.

Meanwhile, at the MTV Music Awards, she wore another colourful sparkling blazer from the Italian fashion house, fully encrusted with Swarovski crystals from its Cruise 2020 collection. She mixed it up with a shimmering pink crop top and Christian Louboutin thigh-high boots.

4. Balmain

Other signature ensembles of Swift come from Balmain, one of the preferred brands of the Kardashian-Jenner clan, including Kim. She presented the disco ball-esque outfit at the 2018 American Music Awards, owning the metallic minidress that’s entirely covered in tiny platinum-mirror-like squares and matching thigh -high boots.Earlier at the 2015 Billboard Music Awards, she appeared in a glittering white jumpsuit with a plunging neckline and cut-out panels at the waist. Additionally, in her 2017 music video “Look What You Made Me Do”, she wore a wrap red dress for a “Snake Queen” look, with snake jewels by Bulgari Serpenti.

5. Louis Vuitton

The “Bejewelled” singer has added Louis Vuitton to her list of favourite designers in recent years.

At the 2016 Met Gala, she tried out an unexpected punk rock look with a silver-scaled minidress from the brand, paired with dark lipstick and lace-up shoes.

Then she totally transformed into a classy lady at her All Too Well: The Short Film premiere at the Toronto International Film Festival last year, thanks to the custom LV gold sequinned gown, complete with chains along the neckline that cascaded onto her chest and shoulders.

6. Julien Macdonald

Swift also shines with Julien Macdonald’s edgy designs.

She made a hit in one of the brand’s sheer gowns with embellishments and sexy cut-outs at the 2016 Country Music Awards as well as the knit minidress at the 2014 Golden Globes Awards. Macdonald’s OTT designs have also been seen on other fashion icons on the red carpet like Jennifer Lopez and Gigi Hadid, per Pink Villa.

7. J. Mendel

Swift also loves looking sweet with J. Mendel’s designs. She wore the fairy tale-like candy pink and yellow silk gown with floral embroidery at the 2019 Time 100 Gala. The billowing sleeves were actually detachable as she removed them later to play the guitar at the event.

The brand has been one of her preferred choices since her early years. She looked like a Greek goddess in a cream and white chiffon gown with thigh-high slit and metallic straps on the neckline at the 2013 Grammy Awards.
