Rock Band Country Track Pack 2 Available Today

Publish date: 2024-04-17

Attention gamers and aspiring country superstars who haven't quite made it past the audition phase for 'American Idol': If you prefer to rock out in the comfort of your living room and in front of your gaming console, then we're happy to remind you that 'Rock Band Country Track Pack 2' hits retail stores today. Live vicariously through country's A-list and play along to top country songs.

'Country Track Pack 2' boasts 21 of the most floor stompin' tunes around and makes use of original masters -- so you know it sounds good.

You can step into the cowboy boots of favorites Keith Urban, Sugarland, Rascal Flatts, Lady Antebellum, Luke Bryan, Darius Rucker, Trace Adkins, Dierks Bentley, Jason Aldean, Chely Wright, Reba McEntire, Shania Twain as well as veterans George Strait, Johnny Cash, Merle Haggard and more!

But please note: The entire track list is available solely through purchasing the 'Rock Band' disc or through a software export code.

The sequel follows the 2009 original version, which features songs like Alan Jackson's 'Good Time,'
Brooks and Dunn's 'Hillbilly Deluxe' and Jason Aldean's 'She's Country.'

You can purchase 'Rock Band Country Track Pack 2' here for Nintendo Wii, and it's also available for Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3.
