Inside Johnny Depp's Island in the Bahamas All the details

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Top stars globally have resorted to owning private Islands. A perfect example is Johnny Depp, an American actor, producer and musician. While filming his first Pirates of the Caribbean film, the famous actor fell in love with the Bahamas. As a result, he acquired a 45-acre tropical private Island in the Caribbean. So, what do you know about Johnny Depp's Island in the Bahamas?

Johnny Depp was pretty affluent before his contentious relationship with Amber Heard. The celebrity lost much money on legal fees and alimony, but the largest financial loss came from lack of work during the Hollywood boycott. Despite all these, Johnny managed to amass quite a property portfolio, including a private Island in the Bahamas.

About Johnny Depp's Island

Johnny Depp's Island, popularly known as Little Halls Pond Cay, is estimated to be 18 hectares (45 acres). Johnny was infatuated with the Island while filming the Pirates of the Caribbean movie and purchased it for about US$3.6 million back in 2004.

According to the Johnny Depp Island map, the Island is located in the Exuma Land and Sea Park protected area of the Bahamas. It was known to be Depp's place to unwind. Whenever he was troubled, he could go back and enjoy the vicinity. Additionally, the American actor wedded his wife Amber Heard on one of its beaches. The property is also known to have hosted the cast crew of the Caribbean series.

Besides various glorious sites, Depp connected with the creatures on the Island to the extent of naming some of them. For instance, he has a friendly Iguana that he named Iggy. The Island also neighbours homes and properties owned by famous celebrities like Eddie Murphy and David Copperfield. These celebrities own Islands off the coast of Nassau.

Pictures of Johnny Depp's Island show just how vast the Island is. Going by the impeccable beaches, it is obvious that the manager, Tara Roberts takes a good turn in ensuring that it is well maintained.

Inside Johnny Depp's Island home

If you are amazed by the vastness of Johnny Depp's Island and how it looks from above, you need to have a sneak preview of its inside. The Island has a ranch-style house with a stunning 360-degree view of the Island. Its entrance has a magnificent main house, two-bedroom yurt, beach house and roundhouse outfitted with a bedroom.

Additionally, it has six beautiful white sand beaches, a lagoon surrounded by palm trees and solar-powered lodgings, including a beach house and a bamboo yurt. The six beaches are named after people very special to him.

He named one beach in honour of his former wife, Vanessa Paradis. Two beaches after his children Lily-Rose Melody and Jack. The remaining beaches are named after his idols, Hunter S. Thompson (Gonzo beach) and Marlon Brando (Brando beach).

Depp acquired a yacht that cost him a whopping $8 million to aid him in accessing the Island. The boat has since been sold due to its high maintenance. Its monthly maintenance expense reportedly costs $400,000 sometimes.

Is Johnny Depp's Island for sale?

No, the Island had already been sold. Johnny sold the Island to JK Rowling in 2016 at $75 million (619 crores). He decided to get rid of it, stating that it troubled him with memories of his ex-wife Amber Heard. JK Rowling is a British author and philanthropist known for her book Harry Potter.
