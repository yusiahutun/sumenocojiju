Vegas actor Taylor Handley has got hitched to his longterm girlfriend Audra Seminaro.

The 29-year-old American actor proposed to his stunning actress and model partner last August.

The pair recently married at the Bates Rincon Del Mar Ranch in California with the bride sharing a snap of the happy event on Twitter.

Man and wife: Taylor Handley is married to his longtime love Audra Seminaro

Audra posed a photo of the newly married couple with the caption:  ‘I love u boo #handleywedding.’

The gorgeous couple looked relaxed as they posed next to a view of rolling Californian hills.

The bride wore a traditional white gown with a strapless, low-cut neckline and a figure-hugging mesh fishtail skirt.

Cute couple: Vegas actor Taylor Handley and longterm love Audra Seminaro have recently wed

She wore her long brunette locks loose and kept her makeup fresh and simple.

Taylor looked like a dream groom with his neatly groomed hair, bow tie and smart black suit with a white rose in his buttonhole. 

Meanwhile, the actor is reportedly due to star in the new USA Network period alien drama Horizon.

Posing up: The couple are pictured at last year's CBS Fall Premiere Party in West Hollywood

Deadline reports that he will play a marine who ‘mysteriously resurfaces after disappearing in a battle over the Pacific which the Navy has deemed classified.’

He is set to star opposite Primeval actress Ruth Bradley, who plays an FBI secretary who investigates a mysterious 'Horizon' project, which is connected to the disappearance of her husband.

Handley plays Lauren's husband, George Howl, who is found alive after vanishing over the Pacific, the details of which have been hidden by the government.

The actor recently starred in the CBS period drama Vegas alongside Dennis Quaid, while he has also starred in The OC, Southland and Dawson's Creek.
